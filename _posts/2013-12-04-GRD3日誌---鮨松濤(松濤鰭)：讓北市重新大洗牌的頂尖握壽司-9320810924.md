<p><span><strong>KUNI編按：</strong></span><br/><span> <strong>現在的我得出來鄭重澄清一下，</strong></span><br/><span> <strong>我已經不會再去松濤鮨了，</strong></span><br/><span> <strong>這家太令人生氣了！！！！</strong></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>-----------</span><br/> <span>松濤鰭(松涛鮨)</span><br/> <span>地址：台北市承德路4段192-1號B1</span><br/> <span>電話：(02)2880-1880</span><br/> <span>時間：12:00~14:00 18:00~22:00</span><br/> <span>-----------</span></p>
<p><span>朋友丟了兩家沒吃過的，說是值得一試的握壽司。</span></p>
<p><span>當下撥了通電話訂中午的位子，店名叫松濤鰭。</span></p>
<p><span>地點在士林區承德路上，嶄新一棟大樓，</span><span>走進地下B1才發現別有洞天。</span></p>
<p><span>(往地下室B1的入口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386153978-338113947.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><span>使用珍貴阿里山千年神檜製作出來的吧檯非常吸睛。</span></p>
<p><span>彎下去仔細一聞，</span></p>
<p><span>除檜木香氣外竟有些許奶味，</span><span>聽說每天早晚都要用熱水與牛奶擦拭跟保養。</span></p>
<p><span>(広部板長)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386153985-403231819.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(阿里山千年神檜製作的壽司檯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386153991-2104363671.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(桌上擺設)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386153998-908869851.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(商午菜單)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154003-537621747.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(阿里山產的山葵)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154008-4231472550.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><span>日籍板長広部祐之來頭不小，(広=廣的漢字)</span></p>
<p><span>乃北市壽司名店磯勢的初代店長，</span></p>
<p><span>後來被高薪挖腳到大陸上海，</span><span>今年九月松濤鰭台北分店開幕時才被派回台灣。</span></p>
<p><span>(台灣極少見的不插電控溫冰箱)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154014-3883677844.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(日籍板長広部祐之)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154020-2092535032.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><span>中午套餐價位較平易近人，NT.1500，</span><span>很適合拿來試水溫。</span></p>
<p><span>內容：</span></p>
<p><span>和風沙拉</span><br/> <span>前付小缽</span><br/> <span>生魚片</span><br/> <span>松茸茶碗蒸</span><br/> <span>握壽司八貫</span><br/> <span>捲物四個</span><br/> <span>湯品</span><br/> <span>果物</span><br/> <span>甜點</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>今天前付是秋刀魚煮，藉醋漬打開胃口大門。</span></p>
<p><span>生魚片則是比目魚，鮪魚赤身及北寄貝。</span></p>
<p><span>赤身沒特別使用刀工，口感意外綿密細緻。</span></p>
<p><span>請負責翻譯的服務生問了広部板長，</span><span>原來使用了本鮪，真的是重本。</span></p>
<p><span>(秋刀魚煮)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154025-805402735.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(比目魚，本鮪赤身與北寄貝)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154031-3111128966.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><span>漬薑片直接擺在珍貴的檜木檯上，</span><span>不禁嚇一跳，原來是故意的。</span></p>
<p><span>連後面八貫握壽司也一樣，</span><span>聽說香氣上有加分效果。</span></p>
<p><span>姑且不論實地品嘗上有何差異，</span></p>
<p><span>但與Sasa那裡動不動拿抹布來騷擾相比，</span><span>松濤這裡吃快樂多啦。</span></p>
<p><span>(直接放在檜木檯上的漬薑片)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 12" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154037-3940917393.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><span>首貫開場白直接鮪魚大腹握。</span></p>
<p><span>聽板長說，是較小隻的本鮪，</span></p>
<p><span>口感上油花豐富過了頭，咀嚼後出汁程度嚇人。</span></p>
<p><span>広部師傅套句朋友講的，手法不屬華麗，</span></p>
<p><span>握壽司的型近乎隨意，程度猶勝野村裕二的隨性。</span></p>
<p><span>但手勁一流，舍利幾乎鬆到極限，</span></p>
<p><span>僅藉米粒上厚厚如麥芽糖般的澱粉黏在一起。</span></p>
<p><span>醋飯與魚料融合極優，若說裕二的舍利同時有著鬆開與化開，</span><span>那広部則較偏向後者。</span></p>
<p><span>(首貫是本鮪大腹)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 13" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154044-1518396769.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><span>後面七貫依序是金目鯛，小肌，軟絲，赤貝，紫海膽，</span><span>穴子與玉子燒。</span></p>
<p><span>(金目鯛)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 14" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154050-3648625871.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(和風沙拉)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 15" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154055-3610255825.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(小肌)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 16" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154062-3324133160.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(軟絲)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 17" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154067-1038627730.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(赤貝)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 18" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154073-3280457883.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><span>其中海膽與星鰻太過絕妙，不得不特別提出來。</span></p>
<p><span>好比軍艦捲使用的海苔並非高檔店常見的丸山，</span><span>未經烘烤，但入口即化，</span></p>
<p><span>加上今天的海膽狀況佳，整體表現太棒了。</span></p>
<p><span>(今天使用的是紫海膽)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 19" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154078-2807020133.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(與海苔融合極優)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 20" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154085-4263216338.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><span>穴子魚淋上比台式醬油膏還黏稠的醬汁。</span></p>
<p><span>應該是久煮多年的老配方，富滿深度的甜。</span></p>
<p><span>搭上如海綿蛋糕鬆軟的魚料與綿黏的舍利，</span><span>令人拍案叫絕。</span></p>
<p><span>(令人拍案叫絕的穴子魚握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 21" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154091-4188322326.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(奢侈的大腹細捲)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 22" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154097-889724426.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><span>由於湯品裡的魚丸是用秋刀做的，</span><span>不禁問師傅是否可單點一貫秋刀握。</span></p>
<p><span>運氣不錯，他說可以。</span></p>
<p><span>來自北海道的魚料時令雖到了尾端，</span></p>
<p><span><strong><span>但仍舊肥美到了極致，甚至比今日的本鮪大腹更為誘人。</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span>(秋刀魚丸湯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 23" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154101-2901702727.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(瓢瓜細捲)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 24" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9320810924/1386154106-2187862624.jpg"/></span></p>

<p><span><strong><span>北部頂尖的江戶前握壽司看來要重新大洗牌了呀！</span></strong></span></p>
