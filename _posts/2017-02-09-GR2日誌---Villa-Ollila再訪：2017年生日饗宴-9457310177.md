<p><span>--------------</span><br/><span>Villa Ollila</span><br/><span>地址：內湖區</span><br/><span>電話：0927-340-034</span><br/><span>--------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>每年生日都要特地挑一家餐廳慶祝，今年亦不例外。</span></p>
<p><span>剛好適逢號稱樂沐私廚的<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/434247239-grd3%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---villa-ollila%EF%BC%9A%E9%AB%98%E7%B4%9A%E7%A7%81%E4%BA%BA%E6%8B%9B%E5%BE%85%E6%9C%83" target="_blank">Villa Ollila</a></strong></span>招待所邀請，</span></p>
<p><span>順理成章就欣然答應了，機會難得嘛。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(又見Continental的仿骨董車)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545887-4218538213.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>晚間抵達時盡是各高級餐廳總監好友們齊聚一堂，</span></p>
<p><span>一段時間未見，大夥兒熱絡擁抱與噓寒問暖一番。</span></p>
<p><strong><span>這幾年台北Fine Dining界人事異動劇烈，</span></strong></p>
<p><strong><span>光這話題就能聊上好幾個鐘頭。</span></strong></p>
<p><span>負責迎賓的是Champagne Perrier Jouet N.V.，</span></p>
<p><span>淺黃的明亮光澤，帶著水果與核果香氣。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(Champagne Perrier Jouet N.V.)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545894-574253021.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(左邊Sam是舊識了，右邊新外場)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545901-3207711779.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(酒杯擺設)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545909-1571610614.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(朋友帶來送我的禮物之一)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545917-951362692.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(要喝的酒杯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545923-2517421566.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(盛宴開始)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545932-1521078014.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>感謝大主廚Dennis為今天發表了全新菜單，</span></p>
<p><span>首道開胃菜便讓我驚艷。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>蕪菁切得極薄，細心包覆極致鮮美的帝王蟹肉，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>一同浸泡於菠菜泥表層，無可挑剔。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(餐前麵包)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545939-2251720268.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(帝王蟹佐蕪菁薄片與菠菜泥)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545946-1842129846.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(可食性植物桌飾)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545953-4263562233.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>第二道是湯品，搭配一支清酒，入口後嚇了一跳，</span></p>
<p><span>甘美純粹尾韻悠長，找來侍酒師端看酒瓶，</span></p>
<p><span>原來是岩手縣的南部美人純米大吟釀。</span></p>
<p><span>鮑魚切得厚實大方，口感相對滑嫩異常，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>昆布湯盡是優雅松露香，味道好，但下得重，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>令我有些擔心後面菜色調味的走向。</strong></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(這支喜歡)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 12" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545961-756466266.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(活鮑佐黑松露與昆布湯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 13" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545965-269840894.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>第三道配酒是Domaine Emile Balland Les Beaux Jours。</span></p>
<p><span>2015年份，有著活潑與魄力的酸度，與鮮美干貝匹配。</span></p>
<p><span>小而美的手摘豆仁負責甜美，羊肚菌一旁點綴淡雅與氣質，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>看似簡單俐落卻充滿廚藝者的細膩見解與品味。</strong></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(Domaine Emile Balland Les Beaux Jours)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 14" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545973-922078452.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(香煎大干貝佐甜豆仁與羊肚菌)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 15" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545980-1225878260.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(充滿美感)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 16" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545982-637397871.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>第四道是西班牙Jamon Serrano煲湯。</span></p>
<p><span>現場由侍酒師將Hennessy VSOP斟入湯裡，入口絕妙。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>法國干邑與西班牙火腿湯頭的組合竟毫不衝突，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>溫潤中帶著來酒香的層次。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>唯一可惜是碗中的黑松露片，存在感較缺。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(斟入法國干邑) </span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 17" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545990-1138770512.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>第五支配酒是Lillet Blanc，草本香氣濃郁。</span></p>
<p><span>主角則是嫩鴨肝，外皮微炙帶點煙燻，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>佐一旁特製開心果餅再度是讓人心儀的組合。</strong></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(香煎鴨肝)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 18" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486545996-822901846.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(Lillet Blanc)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 19" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486546004-1809168187.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(煙燻鴨肝佐漬櫻桃與開心果餅)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 20" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486546008-1936867928.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>第六道主廚冬季沙拉，搭配Stag's Leap Chardonnay。</span></p>
<p><span>喝過幾支Napa谷鹿躍酒廠的CS紅酒，但白酒卻是首次。</span></p>
<p><span>沙拉裡的菜葉種類繁多，連草石蠶的塊莖都有，</span></p>
<p><span>現場淋上熱燙帕馬森起司醬，散著核果與香檳茸香味。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(Stag's Leap Chardonnay)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 21" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486546015-1912978046.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(主廚冬季沙拉)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 22" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486546024-1906111392.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>第七支配酒是國產的Weightstone Musann Blanc，</span></p>
<p><span>初飲訝異，全然的荔枝甜香之下入口卻是強勁白葡萄酒，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>違和到了一個極致，驚人的品質與亮酸，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>台灣造酒技術竟已到這等地步。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>搭配的是現流金目鯛，熟度抓得極好。</span></p>
<p><span>雖然外皮缺了香酥，但魚肉嫩度鮮美動人。</span></p>
<p><span>與首訪相較下，Chef對於熟度掌控又更上一層樓了。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(來自埔里的Weightstone Musann Blanc)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 23" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486546033-612028893.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(現流金目鯛)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 24" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486546038-2851212168.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(精彩熟度)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 25" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486546045-1145252015.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>第八道是非常高檔的羊腿，可惜不能透露太多細節，</span></p>
<p><span>只能說產自某知名山區。</span></p>
<p><span>熟度維持一貫高超，juicy與柔嫩肉質還有細膩馨香。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>才想起來，羊排料理向來最鍾愛台北中山區的Popina，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>羊腿卻還沒吃過這等品質，肌理不粗不柴，味道雋永。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>配酒是Chateau Mas Neuf Chateauneuf-du-Pape，</span></p>
<p><span>花香衝鼻，正值最佳試飲期。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(一整支羊腿)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 26" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486546047-3099329772.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(Chateau Mas Neuf Chateauneuf-du-Pape)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 27" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486546055-725119058.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(小羔羊腿佐韭蔥與發酵黑蒜)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 28" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486546061-1539728467.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(肌理不粗不柴，味道雋永)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 29" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486546068-1579455281.jpg"/> </span></p>

<p><span>(臨時加開朋友送的Denis Thomas Echezeaux)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 30" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9457310177/1486546076-3820027499.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>整餐吃下來最驚豔的是Dennis對於食材的熟度掌控，</span></p>
<p><span>除毫無失誤外，我更喜歡他類似走鋼索般的膽大心細。</span></p>
<p><span>另外侍酒師Sam表現突出，比2015年首訪更沉穩。</span></p>
<p><span>甜點完後是誠品行旅總監與總經理帶來的蛋糕。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>年紀大了，許願時沒想太多，只盼年年健健康康，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>時時與父母或好友們相聚一堂，別無所求。</strong></span></p>
