<p> </p>
<p><span>----------</span><br/><span> 老牌山東水餃大王</span><br/><span> 地址：台北市中山區農安街33號</span><br/><span> 電話：(02)2585-2547</span><br/><span>----------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>最近開始狂吃館子，有一天聊到山東燒雞，</span></span></p>
<p><span>深諳此道的朋友大力狂推老牌山東水餃大王，</span></p>
<p><span>找了天黃道吉日與熱衷台菜的朋友前去。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(典型的台式餐館裝潢)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9353695784/1392843408-3014362024.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span>(小菜檯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9353695784/1392843412-2377728249.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span>共點了：</span></p>
<p><span>炸醬麵(NT.70)，水餃十粒(NT.70)，</span></p>
<p><span>煎餃十五粒(NT.135)，</span><span>酸辣湯(NT.40)還有山東燒雞(NT.250)</span></p>
<p><span>水餃與炸醬麵實地品嚐起來一般，</span></p>
<p><span>倒是煎餃讓人著實吃驚，</span><span>外皮像似裹了乖乖五香粉，油炸過</span><span>。</span></p>
<p><span>酥脆口感多了股陌生的嚼勁，</span><span>味道談不上驚豔，卻印象深刻。</span></p>
<p><span>(炸醬麵)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9353695784/1392843416-4203460108.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(水餃)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9353695784/1392843419-3424358478.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(煎餃)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9353695784/1392843423-1764339762.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(外皮更有嚼勁)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9353695784/1392843427-40882831.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(酸辣湯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9353695784/1392843430-1063233620.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><strong><span>想起以前常去工作室附近一家韓式料理，老闆娘很照顧我，</span></strong></p>
<p><strong><span>每次去吃飯總時特別招待我一份菜單上沒有的私房菜，</span></strong></p>
<p><strong><span>其中一道便是山東燒雞，當時很愛，</span></strong></p>
<p><strong><span>如今回想起來，馬上感到一陣莫名的溫馨。</span></strong></p>
<p><br/><span>(山東燒雞)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9353695784/1392843435-4207181416.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(佈滿金黃色的蒜末)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9353695784/1392843439-891887190.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(濕潤的腿肉及肉凍)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9353695784/1392843443-138765959.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span>今天的山東燒雞讓人不虛此行！</span></p>
<p><span><span><strong>大量醃蒜末滿布在撕開的雞腿肉上，</strong></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><strong>蒜香濃郁，</strong></span></span><span><strong><span>濕潤的肉凍光看就飢腸轆轆。</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span><span><strong>微微醬油配上搶戲的白醋，酸溜溜帶有粗魯的蒜辣。</strong></span></span></p>
<p><span>入味的肉質，不乾不柴比以前吃過的都juicy。</span></p>
<p><span>不見得完全合我胃口，但是吃過最有意思的，</span><span>感謝朋友的推薦。</span></p>
