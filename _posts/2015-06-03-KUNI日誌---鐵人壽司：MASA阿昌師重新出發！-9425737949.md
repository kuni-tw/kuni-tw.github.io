<p><span>----------</span><br/><span>鐵人壽司</span><br/><span>地址：台北市士林區福華路128巷8號</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2837-6688</span><br/><span>時間：11:30~12:50 13:00~14:20 Wed~Mon</span><br/><span>　　　17:30~19:00 19:20~21:00 Wed~Mon</span><br/><span>----------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>自得知前市民大道名店MASA結束營業，</span></p>
<p><span>阿昌師在近天母區的福華路準備另起爐灶時，</span><span>我便開始密切注意日期。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>終於五月三十號正式開幕，</span></p>
<p><span>不論午晚皆有兩梯次。</span></p>
<p><span>訂了中午第二輪，13:00準時到場，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>抵達時才發現忘了帶相機，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>臨時只好跟朋友借手機從權使用(Samsung S5)。</strong></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(門口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310475-2021885181.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(許久不見的阿昌師)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310478-705114135.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(板前)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310482-87427539.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>板前內外似乎沒配合好，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>導致第二輪的客人得在後面乾坐二十幾分鐘。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>仔細一瞧，好像還沒有找到專職的外場。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>從菜單上來看，與MASA時期相比價位較為平實，</span></p>
<p><span>丼飯類定食四百八十起跳。</span></p>
<p><span>點了特上握壽司(NT.980)</span></p>
<p><span>附前菜，Nigiri十貫，味噌，果物及甜品。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(菜單)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310486-3685518042.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>手捲為前鋒！</span></p>
<p><span>外表看似一般，但牛蒡絲切得比一般細，</span></p>
<p><span>炸得爽脆，討好的一道。</span></p>
<p><br/><span>握壽司隨即登場，天使蝦相當巨大，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>黏，軟，溫度適中，與舍利配合緊湊，</strong></span><span><strong>優質。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>金目鯛意外地肥，讚。</span></p>
<p><span>與MASA時期相比，阿昌師在握壽司上面似乎成長不少。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(牛蒡手卷)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310490-4207498217.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(天使蝦握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310494-599149329.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(清酒)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310497-909839680.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(金目鯛握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310501-2778497925.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>微炙干貝夾在海苔裡算常見老梗，</span></p>
<p><span>但入口後便知不同，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>原來裡頭裹著安康魚肝醬，nice！</strong></span></p>
<p><br/><span>北寄貝嫩脆，但這一貫稍嫌可惜，</span></p>
<p><span>除舍利太緊實外，醋飯比例也過頭。</span></p>
<p><span>同時試了一下清口的漬蘿蔔，</span></p>
<p><span>似乎不夠入味，帶著些許青澀。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(干貝佐安康魚肝醬)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310503-2770546325.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(北寄貝握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310506-1036963503.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>突然間一貫本鮪中腹讓人訝異，</span></p>
<p><span>魚生偏肥但不膩，整體水準優。</span></p>
<p><span>蟹肉鮭魚卵與醋飯盛於蘿蔓葉上，</span></p>
<p><span>藉著海膽和樂融融。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(本鮪中腹)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 12" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310509-3381206665.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(蟹肉鮭魚卵與海膽)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 13" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310513-534080709.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><br/><span>微炙的鰈魚鰭邊握與比目魚味道極接近，</span></p>
<p><span>油脂因熱而活化的特徵非常明顯。</span></p>
<p><span>順便試一下朋友的散壽司，</span></p>
<p><span>魚料切大丁，與醋飯咀嚼起來爽快。</span></p>
<p><span>並非不能接受炙燒，</span><strong><span>但此時海鱺與午魚連兩貫都炙燒就多了</span><span>。</span></strong></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(微炙鰈魚鰭邊握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 14" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310520-2284849278.jpg"/> </span></p>

<p><span>(散壽司)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 15" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310524-2494835003.jpg"/> </span></p>

<p><span>(微炙海鱺握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 16" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310528-698009714.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(微炙午魚)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 17" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9425737949/1433310531-892886689.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><br/><span><strong>最後的厚岸生蠔做成Nigiri相當少見，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>但型漂亮，此時藉著一瓶生酒已開始微醺。</span></p>
<p><span>今天的清酒很不錯啊，味道重卻不搶戲。</span></p>

<p><span><strong>今天這一餐吃得舒服許多，</strong></span></p>
