<p><span>-----------------------</span><br/><span>POPINA餐酒館</span><br/><span>地址：台北市中山區南京東路一段13巷6弄1號</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2567-6623</span><br/><span>時間：12:00~14:30 17:45~22:30</span><br/><span>-----------------------</span></p>
<p><br/><span>這一次特地來試<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/451022453-gr2%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---popina%E9%A4%90%E9%85%92%E9%A4%A8%E4%BA%8C%E8%A8%AA%EF%BC%9A%E6%9B%B4%E4%BB%A4%E4%BA%BA%E5%BF%83%E5%84%80%EF%BC%81" target="_blank">Popina</a></strong></span>的Pasta。</span></p>
<p><span>雖然菜單十一月即將更新，但仍按捺不住性子，</span></p>
<p><span>中午再度來到熟悉的巷弄裡。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(菜單)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454176545/1477831266-3725271898.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(漬物)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454176545/1477831276-4173026261.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>午餐吃不多，點一份辣味煙燻培根鵪鶉手工細麵(NT.460)。</span></p>
<p><span>單點的湯品改了料，胭脂蝦與蛤蠣讓人雀躍。</span></p>
<p><span>沒多久麵條上桌，從細心的外觀與擺盤開始期待著。</span></p>
<p><span>兩隻鵪鶉腿與兩隻事先修剪過的鵪鶉翅，熟度極好，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>咬下去瞬間享受外皮的微焦香與腿肉的彈嫩。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>外皮香脆，原來上桌前約略烤過一下，</span></p>
<p><span>確實保留住鵪鶉腿肉的juicy，實在過癮。</span></p>
<p><span>麵體非喜愛的嚼感，可惜。</span></p>
<p><span>醬汁看似味道強烈，其實不然，</span></p>
<p><span>而是秉持著<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/451022453-gr2%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---popina%E9%A4%90%E9%85%92%E9%A4%A8%E4%BA%8C%E8%A8%AA%EF%BC%9A%E6%9B%B4%E4%BB%A4%E4%BA%BA%E5%BF%83%E5%84%80%EF%BC%81" target="_blank">Popina</a></strong></span>一直以來極易辨視的溫和風格，</span></p>
<p><span>與同是餐酒館的<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/449511608-gr2%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---reel%E6%B3%95%E5%BC%8F%E9%A4%90%E9%85%92%E9%A4%A8%E4%BA%8C%E8%A8%AA%EF%BC%9A%E7%BE%8E%E5%91%B3%E5%8D%97%E6%B3%95%E7%87%89%E8%8F%9C" target="_blank">Reel</a></strong></span>大相徑庭。</span></p>
<p><span>情趣雖截然不同，但都很喜歡！</span></p>
