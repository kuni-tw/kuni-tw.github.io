<p> </p>
<p><span>-------------</span><br/><span>鮨小馬 Kunma</span><br/><span>地址：台北市松山區民生東路三段111號B1 (西華飯店)</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2718-1188</span><br/><span>時間：11:30~14:30 18:00~22:00 Mon~Sun</span><br/><span>-------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>沒想到這麼快又回到西華，</span></p>
<p><span>上次停車場惱人的警衛已不在，感謝經理。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>今天是3000的Omakase，數字比預期來得低。</span></p>
<p><span>板前入座，和知san變得稍微不一樣，</span></p>
<p><span>仔細觀察，才發現是多了一股親近。</span></p>
<p><span>以台北所認識的日籍師傅而言相當少見，</span></p>
<p><span>不知為何，板前的他們總是給人距離感。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(板前坐定)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564329-550921764.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(漬蘿蔔片)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564333-3480973278.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(又可以挑清酒杯了)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564336-11346561.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(板前的和知師傅)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564341-319902756.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(二割三分)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564346-2875875322.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>在熱絡對談當中，料理們逐漸登場，</span></p>
<p><span>內容：</span><br/><span>------------</span><br/><span>大根風呂炊</span><br/><span>星鰻苗</span><br/><span>酒粕鴨肝</span><br/><span>水章魚醬油煮</span></p>
<p><span>台灣產鰹魚</span><br/><span>海膽佐海苔</span></p>
<p><span>鮑魚真丈湯</span><br/><span>鰻魚粽</span><br/><span>烤日本太刀</span><br/><span>炸毛蟹白子春捲與河豚</span></p>
<p><span>鮪魚赤身握</span><br/><span>金目鯛握</span><br/><span>鮪魚中腹握</span></p>
<p><span>鰻魚丼</span><br/><span>海瓜子素麵</span></p>
<p><span>白酒櫻花奶酪</span><br/><span>蕎麥冰淇淋</span><br/><span>------------</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>由於事先要求希望以握壽司為主，</span></p>
<p><span>但不知為何內場失誤了，魚生備料不足，</span></p>
<p><span>導致Nigiri一共只出了三貫。</span></p>
<p><span>幸好板長道歉相當誠懇，</span></p>
<p><span>加上料理內容精彩依舊，</span></p>
<p><span>這一餐吃得畢竟還是很開心。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>從大根風呂炊開始活絡味覺，</span></p>
<p><span>煮透的白蘿蔔與味噌，混著雞肉泥球，</span></p>
<p><span>以溫和為主題，讓心暖洋洋。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(大根風呂炊)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564351-4028273853.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>看到星鰻苗時瞬間感到失落，</span></p>
<p><span>這食材不便宜，吃過數次，</span></p>
<p><span>但口感滑順有餘，細嫩卻不足。</span></p>
<p><span>不過和知先生果然厲害，</span></p>
<p><span>藉著不落俗套的調味及處理方式硬是將口感提升不少，</span></p>
<p><span>高明之至。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(星鰻苗)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564354-1427424978.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(漬蘿蔔與小黃瓜)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564359-677200874.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>不知是否誤打誤撞，還是事先有交代會帶清酒的緣故，</span></p>
<p><span>今天的鴨肝與二割三分有如熱戀中情人一般，</span></p>
<p><span>緊密不可分。</span></p>
<p><span>本來較單調的滑膩藉著富有色彩的吟釀，</span></p>
<p><span>竟變更甘甜。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(酒粕鴨肝)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564363-2460016491.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>這一次的章魚煮散發嶄新風貌，</span></p>
<p><span>有別以往的萬巒豬腳濃郁膠質，</span></p>
<p><span>今天的軟爛讓人特別印象深刻。</span></p>
<p><span>來自台灣的鰹魚透露出師傅絕妙的刀工，</span></p>
<p><span>海膽的甜被海苔包覆住，溫潤之後甘味不若以往直接，</span></p>
<p><span>而是含蓄逐漸化至口中。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(水章魚醬油煮)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564368-759467997.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(煙燻蘿蔔)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 12" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564372-2251683001.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(台灣產鰹魚)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 13" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564376-1941873310.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(海膽佐海苔)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 14" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564380-3862699214.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>這當中板前配角們包括漬蘿蔔，煙燻蘿蔔，山藥與漬薑塊等，</span></p>
<p><span>不斷做更迭的動作，</span></p>
<p><span>讓清口成為另一種愉悅的心情。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(山藥)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 15" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564383-3925966500.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(薑塊)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 16" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564387-3950338453.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>和知的拿手好戲真丈湯沒失常，</span></p>
<p><span>與日式第一高湯微酸微甘的調性極相似，</span></p>
<p><span>可惜不夠清澈見底。</span></p>
<p><span>鮑魚切面工整，有預期之中的柔嫩，</span></p>
<p><span>以山椒葉香氣來做最後綴飾似乎已快變例行公事了。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(鮑魚真丈湯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 17" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564392-3818699893.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(和知與台籍二手)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 18" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564396-1494663974.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>鰻魚粽看得出相當費工，熱湯後上桌，</span></p>
<p><span>繼續保持味覺上的節奏。</span></p>
<p><span>太刀魚烤得好，</span></p>
<p><span>美得令人讚嘆的切工下藏不住微微清香的乾淨肉質。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(鰻魚粽)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 19" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564401-158403721.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(中規中矩)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 20" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564404-2611766359.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(小黃瓜)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 21" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564407-406722386.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(烤日本太刀)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 22" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564412-766248298.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>炸春捲裡包覆的是毛蟹白子，</span></p>
<p><span>熱度剛好，酥口中緩緩流出迷人牛奶，</span></p>
<p><span>與細嫩嚼勁的雪白河豚肉合作無間。</span></p>
<p><span>這一道倒是有些可惜，</span></p>
<p><span>不知是否為內場粗心所致，整體不夠盡善盡美。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(炸毛蟹白子春捲與河豚)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 23" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564416-1165025074.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>終於輪到握壽司了，</span></p>
<p><span>三貫以大間一本釣本鮪為主軸，</span></p>
<p><span>在握功上幾乎沒什麼可挑剔之處。</span></p>
<p><span>中腹那一貫可惜了，沒能保持最佳的型。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(大間本鮪一本釣赤身握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 24" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564419-1010090401.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(金目鯛握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 25" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564423-2864264306.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(中腹握可惜了，但還是很好吃！)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 26" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564426-922019050.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>忽然想到，與台北的龍吟極相似，</span></p>
<p><span>和知先生堅持只用大間一本釣高檔貨。</span></p>
<p><span>在成本上是一個相當可怕的壓力，</span></p>
<p><span>尤其這東西近年來愈來愈稀少，</span></p>
<p><span>而幾次以來的經驗，</span></p>
<p><span>並未讓我察覺這奢侈品牌有著高不可攀的味覺優勢。</span></p>
<p><span>當然不可否認，在熟成狀態達到巔峰時，</span></p>
<p><span>一本釣野生本鮪的酸與清香就是乾淨俐落，</span></p>
<p><span>只是這樣等級的美味並非只有大間才做得到而已。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>最後在鰻魚丼與海瓜子素麵下，</span></p>
<p><span>和知的饗宴逐漸接近尾聲，</span></p>
<p><span>蕎麥冰淇淋與令人驚喜的蛋糕劃下唯美句點。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(鰻魚丼)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 27" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564430-766016503.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(海瓜子素麵)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 28" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564433-1806491430.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(蕎麥冰淇淋)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 29" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564437-920109121.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(感謝鮨小馬的蛋糕啊)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 30" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9410534692/1423564442-999583155.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>小馬之所以會讓我近期內迅速回訪第二次，</span></p>
<p><span>理由很簡單："因為它讓我開心。"</span></p>
<p><span>師承神田裕行的顯赫來歷對我來說只據參考價值，</span></p>
<p><span>重點是他對於日式料理那近乎修行者的態度及原則。</span></p>
<p><span>偶爾透過幕簾聽到他在廚房裡相當激動的聲調，</span></p>
<p><span>看得出他對副手們的嚴苛。</span></p>
<p><span>但與他在言談中，那發自內心的禮貌與謙遜，</span></p>
<p><span>絕非來自傲慢的做作，太讓人欣賞了。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><strong>三訪指日可待！</strong></span></p>
