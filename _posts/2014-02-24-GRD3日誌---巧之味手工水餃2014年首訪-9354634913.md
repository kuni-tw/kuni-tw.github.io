<p><span>自<span><strong><a href="https://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/339189245-grd3%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---%E7%BE%A9%E8%81%9A%E6%9D%B1%E5%8A%89%E5%AE%B6%E6%B0%B4%E9%A4%83%EF%BC%9A%E5%A5%BD%E5%90%83%E4%B8%8D%E9%81%8E%E9%A4%83%E5%AD%90" target="_blank"><span>義聚東</span></a></strong></span>後開始對水餃產生了興趣，<a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/339189245-grd3%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---02012014-%E7%BE%A9%E8%81%9A%E6%9D%B1%E5%8A%89%E5%AE%B6%E6%B0%B4%E9%A4%83%EF%BC%9A%E5%A5%BD%E5%90%83%E4%B8%8D%E9%81%8E" target="_blank"><br/></a></span></p>
<p><span>連續尋訪幾家，其中包括網友推薦的巧之味，位於北市濟南路上。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(店門口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9354634913/1393267117-2480636018.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span>從新生南一段在麥當勞路口轉進一路直行，不一會兒就到。</span></p>
<p><span>2005開幕至今店面不算老舊，台菜裝潢明亮簡潔。</span></p>
<p><span>一共點了酸辣湯(NT.30)，招牌水餃(NT.5/粒)，</span></p>
<p><span>韭菜水餃(NT.5/粒)與干貝水餃(NT.7/粒)</span></p>
<p><span>(店內)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9354634913/1393267121-2892390630.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span>或許是</span><strong><span>期待過高</span></strong><span>，今天這一行有些失望。</span></p>
<p><span>酸辣湯大碗，料豐富但口味平淡。</span></p>
<p><span>畢竟不是特別愛喝這種東西，大概是學生時期太常吃吧，</span><span>後來開始反感了。</span></p>
<p><span>(小菜)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9354634913/1393267126-279562820.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><span>(酸辣湯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9354634913/1393267131-634125222.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(大份且平價)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9354634913/1393267136-2219827500.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><span>巧之味的水餃皮偏厚，口感較紮實。</span></p>
<p><span>看得出有下功夫，可惜非我所愛。</span></p>
<p><span>個人依舊屬意<strong><span>永和義聚東的層次與Q勁</span></strong>，</span><span>雖然較貴但尺寸也大上不少。</span></p>
<p><span>(招牌與韭菜水餃)　</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9354634913/1393267142-278119058.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(韭菜內餡)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9354634913/1393267146-618061733.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><span>干貝餃是綠色的，</span><span>內餡裡據說包覆珠貝，</span></p>
<p><span>餃皮據說有使用海藻，但實地上嚐不出來。</span></p>
<p><strong><span>所以繼續踏上尋找美好的餃子之旅吧。</span></strong></p>
