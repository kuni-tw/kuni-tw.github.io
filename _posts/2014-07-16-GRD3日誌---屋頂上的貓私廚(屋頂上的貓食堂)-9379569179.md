<p> </p>
<p><span>---------------</span><br/><span>屋頂上的貓私廚(屋頂上的貓食堂)</span><br/><span>地址：台北市信義區逸仙路32巷9號一樓</span><br/><span>電話：0961-173-451</span><br/><span>---------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>屋頂上的貓私廚網路上聲名遠播，老早就心癢，</span></p>
<p><span>某天晚上總算有機會進去一睹廬山真面目。</span></p>
<p><span>走在黑暗的逸仙路巷弄間，門口低調只打上R與P兩個大寫字母，</span></p>
<p><span>代表的是Roy與胖胖兩位主人。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453441-3337149694.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>打著私廚名義賣的是主人居家的私房料理，</span></p>
<p><span>走進些微凌亂的客廳就是感到不習慣。</span></p>
<p><span>不過仔細一瞧，看似雜物的東西雖多卻擺得井然有序，</span></p>
<p><span>是非常乾淨且舒服的用餐環境。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(相當居家的用餐環境)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453444-4084346207.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(餐盤)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453447-4274430643.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(客廳)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453450-2265932848.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(開始製作開胃小點)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453452-2988127231.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>今日用餐價格為一人<span><strong>NT.1800</strong></span>，</span></p>
<p><span>由於並非什麼高級餐廳所以價位不低，</span></p>
<p><span>滿心期待那些省下來的人事及裝潢成本能盡情發揮在食物上面。</span></p>
<p><span>不過隨著料理持續上桌，失落感變得明顯。</span></p>
<p><span>餐點內容：</span></p>
<p><span>----------------------------------------------------</span><br/><span>開胃小點</span><br/><span>豬肝蘿蔔絲沙拉</span><br/><span>蔥燒豬肋排佐三星蔥醬</span><br/><span>麻油雞麵線佐佛手瓜</span><br/><span>甜菜根果汁</span><br/><span>奶油乾煎舫魚佐烤番茄</span><br/><span>南杏西瓜排骨湯</span><br/><span>香蕉派佐太妃糖奶油</span><br/><span>----------------------------------------------------</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>餐前酒是Sutter Home的Moscato，</span></p>
<p><span>開胃小點嚴格來論並未有何亮眼表現。</span></p>
<p><span>下一道豬肝沙拉在溫度上過高了，</span></p>
<p><span>另外少了裝盤上的細心與食材的搭配就是讓人感到不滿。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(Moscato)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453454-187485613.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(開胃小點)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453457-3020466825.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(右邊那位是老闆之一：一點也不胖的胖胖)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453459-3570773874.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(豬肝蘿蔔絲沙拉)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453461-414691595.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>主餐登場時令人失望，怎麼會是豬肋排與麻油雞麵線呢？</span></p>
<p><span>由於Roy主廚有親自出來說明，今天確定由他來掌廚。</span></p>
<p><span>不愧經驗老到，肋排熟度拿捏相當優質，</span></p>
<p><span>口感上沒什麼好挑剔。</span></p>
<p><span>麻油雞的醬汁屬爽口清淡型，使用佛手瓜搭配則讓人疑惑。</span></p>
<p><span>此時一旁強烈的果汁機聲響過於吵雜，</span></p>
<p><span>沒多久甜菜根果汁上桌，清爽甜美。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(蔥燒豬肋排佐三星蔥醬)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453463-1836829590.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(麻油雞麵線佐佛手瓜)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 12" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453466-512042492.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(甜菜根果汁)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 13" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453469-1319824652.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>最後一道主餐煎舫魚熟度不錯，</span></p>
<p><span>但若嚴格檢視的話嫩度與細緻度則略乏。</span></p>
<p><span>然後當湯品上桌時忽然間我真的沒辦法了，</span></p>
<p><span>裡頭放的竟然是西瓜皮，味道也...非常西瓜。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(奶油乾煎舫魚佐烤番茄)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 14" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453473-2508206304.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(舫魚熟度不錯，可惜嫩度與細緻度則略乏。)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 15" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453475-1920927758.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(唉，西瓜皮排骨湯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 16" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9379569179/1405453477-2618597318.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>甜品總算讓人有愉悅的心情，</span></p>
<p><span>太妃糖與輕盈的鮮奶油漂亮地結合一起，</span><span>擺在甜膩適中的香蕉派上頭。</span></p>
<p><span>不論溫度甜度及口感都恰好，這味道連男人都愛吃。</span></p>
<p><span>但今晚的一千八配上如此等級的主餐，</span><span>錢花得很心疼呀。(長嘆)</span></p>
<p> </p>
