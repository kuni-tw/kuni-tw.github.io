<p><span>---------------</span><br/><span>添財日本料理(添財壽司屋)</span><br/><span>地址：台北市中正區武昌街一段16巷6號(武昌總店)</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2361-5119</span><br/><span>時間：11:00~14:30 17:00~23:00</span><br/><span>---------------</span><span><br/></span></p>
<p><br/><span><strong>一家幾十年的老牌日本料理，</strong></span><span>聽過朋友提過幾次，早就心癢。</span></p>
<p><span>這一天中午臨時訂了位，得償所願。</span></p>
<p><span>位在北車對面武昌街窄小的巷弄裡，</span><span>走進去在吧檯前坐定，</span><span>單點三樣：</span></p>
<p><span>上等生魚片(NT.400)</span></p>
<p><span>上等握壽司(NT.400)</span></p>
<p><span>蝦天婦羅(NT.280)</span></p>
<p><span>(巷弄間的門口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426598928/1434402992-1831732431.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(吧檯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426598928/1434403006-4007213292.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(玻璃櫃)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426598928/1434403013-2939296095.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><span>天婦羅相當好，米白色的炸衣色澤美麗，</span><span>蔬菜漂亮。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>兩隻蝦，鮮嫩，保留大量肉汁，</strong></span><span>入口後很享受。</span></p>
<p><span>炸衣偏厚算是美中不足，</span><span>但以這價位而言算高明了。</span></p>
<p><span>(炸蝦天婦羅)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426598928/1434403019-3445645446.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span>握壽司</span><span>一共六貫，包括：</span></p>
<p><span>黑點，石鯛，石老，赤貝，比目魚鰭邊與鮭魚。</span></p>
<p><span>炙燒占了一半一上，並非不能接受，</span><span>反正也沒事先要求。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>但舍利緊實過頭，簡直類似迴轉壽司，</strong></span><span><strong>看來這非添財強項。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>(上等握壽司)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426598928/1434403025-2859573338.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(赤貝，微炙比目魚鰭邊，炙燒鮭)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426598928/1434403034-2836707795.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span>生魚片上桌，</span><span>再度又給了炙燒鮭，無妨。</span></p>
<p><span>迦納偏瘦，算了，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>但紅甘微微腥味就讓人不爽了。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>那是很容易讓人辨別的氣味，魚市場裡常聞到。</span></p>
<p><span>以刀工而言，切得整齊，</span><span>同時看得出師傅下刀俐落，相當有經驗。</span> </p>
<p><span>(上等生魚片)</span></p>
