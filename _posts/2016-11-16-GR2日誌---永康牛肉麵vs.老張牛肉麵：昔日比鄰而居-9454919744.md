<p><span>------------------</span><br/><span>永康牛肉麵</span><br/><span>地址：台北市大安區金山南路二段31巷17號</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2351-1051</span><br/><span>時間：11:00~15:30 16:30~21:00</span></p>
<p><span>川味老張牛肉麵店(永康總店)</span><br/><span>地址：台北市大安區愛國東路105號</span><br/><span>電話：(02) 2396 0927</span><br/><span>時間： 11:00~15:00 17:00~21:00</span><br/><span>-------------------</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><br/><span><strong>其實原本只打算寫永康牛肉麵的，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>不過想了一想，最後決定將兩篇併一起。</strong></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>好幾年沒去，這一行特別有感觸，</span></p>
<p><span>當然主要也是兩禮拜以來連續吃好幾家牛肉麵的關係。</span></p>
<p><span>除癮頭剛好，另外只是想趁記憶猶新比較比較。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>永康知名度高，下去四點多才開門便已人滿為患，</span></p>
<p><span>好在不用排隊，不然提早去的如意算盤可白打了。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(下午剛開店已滿座)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454919744/1479170213-2361098217.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(菜單)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454919744/1479170221-1084937120.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><br/><span>用餐內容：</span><br/><span>------------------</span><br/><span>牛肉麵(小，NT.220)</span><br/><span>粉蒸排骨(NT.120)</span><br/><span>粉蒸肥腸(NT.120)</span><br/><span>------------------</span></p>
<p><br/><span><strong>肥腸與排骨真的好吃，熱燙端來，肥嫩不膩，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>明顯的花椒香。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>向來不太吃辣，一兩口額頭上滲出斗大汗珠，</span></p>
<p><span>但辣得過癮，辣得爽快，底層番薯來得適時。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>應該是這一兩年來最重油重鹹的牛肉麵了。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>麵條嚼勁足，可惜就是不愛細麵。</span></p>
<p><span>肉塊尺寸大，量多，但部分肌理過粗甚至偏柴咬不斷。</span></p>
<p><span>點的是小碗，但竟然無法完食，</span></p>
<p><span>湯頭非常厚重夠味，情不自禁續了一次。</span></p>
<p><span>這裡酸菜切得細，鹹度高，入湯後是另一番滋味，</span></p>
<p><span>喜歡。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(兩籠粉蒸)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454919744/1479170229-3431061017.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(紅燒牛肉麵)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454919744/1479170240-945508628.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(酸菜)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454919744/1479170248-2517705764.jpg"/> </span></p>

<p><span>(肉塊尺寸大)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454919744/1479170257-2195548229.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(可惜部分肌理過粗咬不斷)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454919744/1479170264-3190465171.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(夠味的湯頭又續一次)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454919744/1479170271-3652468050.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><strong>吃完後當然不能放過老張，畢竟是行動派，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>幾天後迅速成行。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>兩家聽說原本是鄰居，2009才搬至如今愛國麗水街口，</span></p>
<p><span>生意看起來還好，無法跟永康比。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(老張門口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454919744/1479170196-3835820528.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>用餐內容：</span><br/><span>------------------</span><br/><span>牛肉麵(小，NT.200)</span><br/><span>粉蒸排骨(NT.110)</span><br/><span>粉蒸肥腸(NT.110)</span><br/><span>小菜(NT.50)</span><br/><span>------------------</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(小菜自取)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454919744/1479170117-4182108588.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(拿了一盤小菜)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 12" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454919744/1479170124-3276129349.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><br/><span>粉蒸兩籠調味與永康相似，底層亦鋪一層地瓜，</span></p>
<p><span>但昔日鄰居顯然入味許多，辣度更出色。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>惱人的是老張粉蒸裡頭部分竟然是溫涼的，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>內場太馬虎了吧。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>牛肉麵湯頭初嘗倒是一般，不過順口耐喝。</span></p>
<p><span>菜單上選辣味，但實際僅有微辣，一旁酸菜更是吃不慣。</span></p>
<p><span>肉塊比永康好，一咬便無負擔地斷開，</span></p>
<p><span>麵條不算彈但還算帶點筋性。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(粉蒸兩籠)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 13" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454919744/1479170132-3034970449.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(不太夠味的辣味牛肉麵)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 14" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9454919744/1479170139-1428646817.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>整體來說老張價格比死對頭稍微便宜一點，</span></p>
<p><span>但個人偏向永康太多。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>或許稱不上心頭愛，又重油重鹹了些，不過起碼對味。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>配上不錯的酸菜與熱燙粉蒸兩籠，蒸氣四益，</span></p>
<p><span>一個大男人下午沒事跑去過過紅燒癮，邊吃邊不停拭汗，</span></p>
<p><span>太爽了。</span></p>
