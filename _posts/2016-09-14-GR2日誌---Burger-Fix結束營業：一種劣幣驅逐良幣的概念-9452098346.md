<p><span>-------------------------</span><br/><span>Burger Fix</span><br/><span>地址：台北市大安區安和路一段21巷5號</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2751-6628</span><br/><span>時間：11:30~21:30</span><br/><span>-------------------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>15號是Burger Fix最後一天營業，</span></p>
<p><span>特地趕今天14號到訪。</span></p>
<p><span>之前由於聽過不少負面評語所以躊躇沒來，</span></p>
<p><span>但這幾天老饕朋友們對這家漢堡依依不捨，</span></p>
<p><span>FB裡紛紛放送消息，連<span><strong>劣幣驅逐良幣</strong></span>的概念都用上，</span></p>
<p><span>心想這下不去不行了。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(門口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="/assets/images/9452098346/1473859020-595198487.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>點雙層起士牛肉堡組合餐(NT.395)，</span></p>
<p><span>飲料外可選薯格或薯條。</span></p>
<p><span>一旁一位阿姨不停推薦薯格，讓我有些感動，</span></p>
<p><span>應該是長久以來的忠實熟客吧，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>一邊嘮叨再也吃不到美味漢堡怎麼辦。</strong></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(內部裝潢)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="/assets/images/9452098346/1473859030-8295720.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(桌位)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="/assets/images/9452098346/1473859041-1547393881.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(菜單)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="/assets/images/9452098346/1473859050-494458213.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>Burger Fix的牛肉漢堡應該是吃過肉味最重的，</span></p>
<p><span>不愧是<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/414466777-a-cut%E7%89%9B%E6%8E%92%E9%A4%A8%EF%BC%9A90%E5%A4%A9%E4%B9%BE%E5%BC%8F%E7%86%9F%E6%88%90%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2%E8%82%8B%E7%9C%BC" target="_blank">A Cut</a></strong></span>牛排館的關係企業。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>不屬油脂豐厚或入口潤滑細膩，乃是厚重炙燒香，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>藉微焦外層帶來絕佳牛肉風味。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>但麵包不行，沒咬幾口已擋不住來自肉汁的攻勢，</span></p>
<p><span>手上瞬間油膩滿點，實在狼狽不堪，是我非常在意的點。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(漢堡組合餐)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="/assets/images/9452098346/1473859068-1580667522.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(雙層起士牛肉堡)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="/assets/images/9452098346/1473859076-3331169634.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(肉味美妙)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="/assets/images/9452098346/1473859086-1657476285.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>近幾年由牛排界轉戰漢堡圈的例子愈來愈多，</span></p>
<p><span>好比教父體系的<a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/436177111-burger-ray-%E9%B4%A8%E8%82%9D-%E9%B5%9D%E8%82%9D" target="_blank"><strong><span>Burger Ray</span></strong></a>，或自<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/414466777-a-cut%E7%89%9B%E6%8E%92%E9%A4%A8%EF%BC%9A90%E5%A4%A9%E4%B9%BE%E5%BC%8F%E7%86%9F%E6%88%90%E6%BE%B3%E6%B4%B2%E8%82%8B%E7%9C%BC" target="_blank">A Cut</a></strong></span>的Burger Fix。</span></p>
<p><span>前幾天的<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/451939088-gr2%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---burger-lab%EF%BC%9A%E7%BE%8E%E5%91%B3%E7%9A%84%E6%BC%86%E9%BB%91%E6%BC%A2%E5%A0%A1" target="_blank">Burger Lab</a></strong></span>雖不算出自正統牛排館，</span></p>
<p><span>但天成飯店西餐總主廚JJ對牛排亦算熟練，</span></p>
<p><span>加上在異國美食上涉獵極廣，所以嘗試較創新的路線。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong><span>或許人總容易對即將逝去的事物感到美好，</span></strong></p>
<p><strong><span>同時淡化缺陷，使得回憶充滿不捨與感傷，</span></strong></p>
<p><strong><span>成為無窮的思念與逐漸封存的故事，</span></strong></p>
<p><strong><span>永存心底。</span></strong></p>
