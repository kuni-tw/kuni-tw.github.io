<p><span>------------------------</span><br/><span>鍋媽媽牛奶火鍋</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2531-4598</span><br/><span>地址：台北市中山區天祥路6號</span><br/><span>時間：11:00~14:00 17:00~22:00 Tue~Sun</span><br/><span>------------------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>鼎鼎大名的鍋媽媽吃過一次就印象深刻了，</span></p>
<p><span>價位偏高，<strong><span>但濃郁不膩的牛奶鍋非常耐煮，</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span>很合胃口。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(店門口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9349089914/1390892803-3929266607.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>最近幫朋友的公司訂鍋媽媽尾牙，</span></p>
<p><span>才想起有一段時間沒來了，便也給自己訂了位，</span></p>
<p><span>晚間抵達後只見店內人滿為患，生意好得嚇人。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><span>與陳老闆算是車友，曾幫他訂過餐廳，</span></span></p>
<p><span>深知他對於美食的執著猶勝身邊不少饕客級好友。</span></p>
<p><span>今天他推薦<strong><span>去</span><span>骨嫩雞腿鍋套餐</span></strong>(NT.420)與<strong><span>9+的和牛牛小排</span></strong>(NT.1600)，</span></p>
<p><span>他說狀況不錯，便各點一份，牛奶鍋底是必選。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(菜單)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9349089914/1390892808-1481965090.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(菜盤)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9349089914/1390892814-1608998018.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(服務生幫你調製沾醬)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9349089914/1390892820-194669979.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>充斥傳統台式服務的鍋媽媽在裝潢上或許沒有日式涮涮鍋來得精緻，</span></p>
<p><span><strong><span>但陳老闆在食材上的品味與見解絕對不容置疑，</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span>沒事看他盡往日本跑遍當地美食，</span></p>
<p><span>最難得的是他那極為謙虛與好學的求知心態。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>今天的去骨雞腿夠嫩，沒什麼好挑剔。</span></p>
<p><span>吃到一半舀口牛奶湯頭，濃郁度不缺，</span></p>
<p><span>香氣讓我聯想到永和知名的好康九九。</span></p>
<p><span>問了老闆，果不其然，配方只差沒用麵粉下去炒過，</span></p>
<p><span>其他材料則差不多，詳細部分沒多問，畢竟是商業機密。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(久煮不膩的牛奶湯頭)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9349089914/1390892826-3269245858.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(冬瓜茶不錯喝)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9349089914/1390892831-387898245.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(本日單點)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9349089914/1390892838-1781164359.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(去骨嫩雞腿)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9349089914/1390892845-954860645.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>9+和牛吃過多次，價昂不用說，</span></p>
<p><span><strong><span>綿密有如大理石般的油花，像白雪一樣撒在橘紅色肉片上，</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span>美麗得連隔壁桌都不禁望過來讚嘆。</span></p>
<p><span>柔軟肉質有如棉絮，用筷子夾進湯鍋裡涮時真得小心翼翼，</span></p>
<p><span>先吃原味然後試試老闆特調芝麻醬，</span></p>
<p><span>微酸微甜中帶點美乃滋濃稠與胡麻香，堪稱一絕。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(9+和牛牛小排狀況不錯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9349089914/1390892850-2868606329.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(肉質柔軟，油花有如大理石般)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9349089914/1390892855-2239746940.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(美味的一刻！)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 12" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9349089914/1390892860-3673545497.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>頂級和牛用涮的果然好吃，</span></p>
<p><span>不過比起燒肉或是炭烤在滋味上似乎還是略輸一籌。</span></p>
<p><span>但海鮮可不一樣了，今晚單點活蝦令人驚豔，</span></p>
<p><span>活蹦亂跳的拍照時根本無法對焦。</span></p>
<p><span>大干貝更不遑多讓，是生食等級的好貨，</span></p>
<p><span>涮下去不論五分還是全熟都超級好吃。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(生食等級的大干貝)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 13" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9349089914/1390892866-375981106.jpg"/> </span></p>

<p><span>(甜點)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 18" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9349089914/1390892898-478249998.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>鍋媽媽或許在價位上不是那麼平易近人，</span></p>
<p><span>但那<strong><span>耐吃的牛奶湯頭，久煮不膩口，</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span>加上老饕級的老闆在背後把關，</span></p>
<p><span>難怪生意強強滾，沒事先訂位根本進不來呀。</span></p>
