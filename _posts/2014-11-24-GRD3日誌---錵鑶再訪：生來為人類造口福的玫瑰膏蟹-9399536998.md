<p><span>(過癮又滿滿的蟹膏，這東西根本是生來為人類造口福的吧！)</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>----------------</span><br/><span>錵鑶 日本料理</span><br/><span>地址：新北市永和區中正路90號</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2942-5738</span><br/><span>時間：11:00~14:00 17:00~21:30 Tue~Sun</span><br/><span>----------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>其實已經不算是再訪，</span></p>
<p><span>自首訪後還來過多次，只是沒時間寫。</span></p>
<p><span>通常都坐板前，</span></p>
<p><span>然後問聖凱有什麼特殊的好料單點。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>這一次與挑嘴的朋友們相約，特地事先訂位，</span></p>
<p><span>Omakase預算是一千。</span></p>
<p><span>從牛蒡蘋果手卷開始暖身，</span></p>
<p><span>今天負責當綠葉的是久保田純米大吟釀，</span></p>
<p><span>可惜好像不是那麼稱職。</span></p>
<p><span>接下來是鱈魚白子，藉著酒蒸淡化腥味，</span></p>
<p><span>份量給得很足，爽快。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(牛蒡蘋果手卷)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757538-783136608.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(久保田)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757545-2570953880.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(酒蒸鱈魚白子)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757549-3129456396.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>三貫生魚片依序是：</span></p>
<p><span>劍旗魚佐魚子醬</span><br/><span>青甘肚</span><br/><span>黃雞魚肚</span></p>
<p><span>切工依舊平穩，魚生溫度適中，</span></p>
<p><span>這也是為何我多次再訪錵鑶的原因之一。</span></p>
<p><span>在板前的聖凱不算技巧高超或是華麗炫目，</span></p>
<p><span>但他讓人放心。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(劍旗魚佐魚子醬)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757554-1385520388.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(青甘肚)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757559-1519600763.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(黃雞魚肚)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757566-3356150982.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>Nigiri隨之而來，</span></p>
<p><span>由於知道我很愛漬鮪魚，一道來自傳統江戶前料理的壽司，</span></p>
<p><span>所以他嘗試複製一貫放進菜單。</span></p>
<p><span>黃鰭鮪赤身狀況不錯，可惜醃漬不夠到位，</span></p>
<p><span>推測是刀工問題，沒適切斷開魚肉裡的肌理，</span></p>
<p><span>口感因此缺了綿密，連帶影響入味。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(紅甘肚握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757570-3306194583.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(漬鮪魚赤身握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757575-1670092904.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(微炙大干貝握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757579-1190848403.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>藍蝦佐海膽讓人欣喜，</span></p>
<p><span>上頭一點魚子醬為配色添亮點。</span></p>
<p><span>馬糞海膽只要等級好，入口就是濃郁甘甜，</span></p>
<p><span>與軟膩帶點Q勁的蝦肉，一同於口中化開。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(藍蝦海膽握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757585-65816172.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>微炙比目魚鰭邊不用說，已算是聖凱駕輕就熟的的拿手好戲。</span></p>
<p><span>從魚生挑選與備料上就看出細心，</span></p>
<p><span>微炙燒過的清香油脂與醋飯合拍。</span></p>
<p><span>這貫握壽司現在在北市常見，</span></p>
<p><span>但做得好的真的不多，除錵鑶外個人也愛游壽司的。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(炙燒比目魚鰭邊握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 12" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757590-327387646.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>今天最大的驚喜就是玫瑰膏蟹，</span></p>
<p><span>聖凱拿在手上在板前炫耀，一旁客人全都目不轉睛，</span></p>
<p><span>這東西從沒看過也沒吃過，個頭很大。</span></p>
<p><span>不算活繃亂跳，但眼神看起來有點兇。</span></p>
<p><span>先拿進廚房裡處理，同時上一份厚切烤圓鱈下巴，</span></p>
<p><span>肉質不用說比一般鱈魚來得細膩滑順，好吃！</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(吼，拿螃蟹來炫耀一番)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 13" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757594-1410875514.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(烤圓鱈下巴)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 14" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757599-813895645.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><br/><span>好久沒有遇到鵝肝，</span></p>
<p><span>最近在法式餐廳或高檔牛排館裡吃到的都是鴨肝，</span></p>
<p><span>實在他媽的不爽。</span></p>
<p><span>日式料理的錵鑶竟願意進這樣一塊好料，功德無量啊。</span></p>
<p><span>與漬鮭魚卵做成小缽稍微填補一下肚子至此已所剩不多的空間。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(好大一塊進口鵝肝)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 15" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757604-1950587850.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(迷你鮭魚卵鵝肝丼)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 16" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757606-33232395.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>終於<strong>玫瑰膏蟹</strong>登場，要不是親眼所見，</span></p>
<p><span>不然真難以相信世上竟然有這種螃蟹，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>根本是生來為人類造口福的。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>眼見所及均是滿滿艷麗橙黃蟹膏，完全不枉玫瑰稱號。</span></p>
<p><span>肉質部分沒毛蟹來得細膩與鮮甜，</span></p>
<p><span>但奢侈將整塊蟹膏塞入口後，</span></p>
<p><span>過癮程度只能用"<strong>難以想像的爽</strong>"來形容。</span></p>
<p><span>這東西數量不多，進貨不穩，</span></p>
<p><span>所以想吃得碰運氣，立馬跟聖凱預留下一隻。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(這蟹膏太讓人幸福啦)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 17" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757610-1998542581.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(原來味噌湯換了)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 18" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757614-713394609.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(甜點水果)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 19" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9399536998/1416757618-3739833064.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>這又是一次令人難忘的晚餐啊，</span></p>
<p><span>付完帳走出店門，</span></p>
<p><span>日間教課的疲倦已不見蹤影，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>人生真是太美好了。</strong></span></p>
