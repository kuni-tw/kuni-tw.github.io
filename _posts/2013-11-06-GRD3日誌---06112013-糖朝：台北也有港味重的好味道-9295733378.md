<p><br/><span>----------------------------------------------------------------</span><br/><span>　糖朝</span><br/><span>　地址：台北市忠孝東路四段201號二樓</span><br/><span>　電話：(02)2772-2889</span><br/><span>　時間：24h</span><br/><span>----------------------------------------------------------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>　原本是為了找尋暖冬的甜點而去，</span></p>
<p><span>　卻意外發現台北也有港味重的好味道。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>　</span><br/><span>　下午趁客人不多時來到有夠難停車的東區統領，</span></p>
<p><span>　從星巴克對面的門口，順著幽暗樓梯走到二樓一下子豁然開朗。</span></p>
<p><span>　裡頭空間寬敞，氣氛不錯但質感稍不足。</span></p>
<p><span>　由於肚子餓，決定順便試試鹹食。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>　(二樓的門口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9295733378/1383748099-3912709258.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>　(餐廳內空間寬敞)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9295733378/1383748106-416028058.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>　</span></p>
<p><span>　(甜點區)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9295733378/1383748112-2040919069.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>　(備用空間)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9295733378/1383748119-341559310.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>　</span><br/><span>　</span><br/><span>　看到菜單上有乾炒牛河，二話不說馬上點一份，</span></p>
<p><span>　另外還有秘製腸粉，北菇蘿蔔糕，腐皮鮮蝦捲，</span></p>
<p><span>　甜點不用說，點了某大飯店經理推薦的椰汁香芋紫米露與原木桶豆花。</span></p>
<p><span>　</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>　(菜單)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9295733378/1383748127-398264357.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>　</span><br/><span>　由於在香港吃過經典的干炒牛河，</span></p>
<p><span>　那薄中帶勁的河粉，透出熱呼呼的鑊氣，</span></p>
<p><span>　牛肉又嫩又入味，香氣十足油而不膩，吃過一次便令人永生難忘。</span></p>
<p><span>　可惜在台灣別說接近的，連像樣的也沒嚐過。</span></p>
<p><span>　台北糖朝的師傅今天看樣子狀況不錯，稱不上猛火但熱氣夠逼人，</span></p>
<p><span>　河粉薄度不錯，牛肉嫩度佳，雖未臻完美但已讓我十分開心了。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>　(應該是台北吃過最佳的乾炒牛河)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9295733378/1383748133-2136925232.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>　今天的蘿蔔糕熱度不夠，算失誤一次，</span></p>
<p><span>　糖朝畢竟是高價位，不得不放大檢視，</span></p>
<p><span>　但由於有反映給服務生，所以結帳時才發現這一道不用算錢，</span></p>
<p><span>　加上副店長頻頻道歉讓人感到都不好意思了，</span></p>
<p><span>　看來香港的強勢服務到了台北還是得入境隨俗呀。</span></p>
<p><span>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>　(北菇蘿蔔糕)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9295733378/1383748138-4017322395.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>

<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9295733378/1383748144-2188238861.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>　(秘製腸粉)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9295733378/1383748150-41603883.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><br/><span>　最後，特地來品嘗的甜點完全沒讓人失望，</span></p>
<p><span>　紫米粥椰香芬芳，口感順滑，表現中上。</span></p>
<p><span>　原木桶豆花則讓人驚豔，些許偏甜但並無大礙，</span></p>
<p><span>　重點是豆香濃郁至極，隨著熱氣直衝腦門，</span></p>
<p><span>　<strong>一想到在冬天可以吃到這樣的東西，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>　<strong>心頭便慢慢暖了起來。</strong></span></p>
