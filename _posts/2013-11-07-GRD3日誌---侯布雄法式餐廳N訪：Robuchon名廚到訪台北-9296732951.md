<p><span>(與Robuchon大師合照)</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>---------------</span><br/><span> L'ATELIER de Joël Robuchon</span><br/><span> 地址：台北市松仁路28號五樓</span><br/><span> 電話：(02)8729-2628</span><br/><span> 時間：11:30~21:30</span><br/><span>---------------</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><span>世界名廚Robuchon先生十月下旬親臨台北。</span></span></p>
<p><span>晚宴一下子被訂滿，得知中午有限定套餐，</span><span>順便幫老爸老媽也訂了位。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><span>(感謝大師幫我簽名)</span></span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9296732951/1383804481-3942089368.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>這家吃過幾次，首訪時，侍酒師與松露菜色讓人不是很滿意。</span></span></p>
<p><span>不過大部分的料理水準都非常優，尤其是那均衡又細緻的創意。</span></p>
<p><span>中午提早抵達Bellavita地下停車場，然後逛逛樓上精品。</span></p>
<p><span>到五樓門口就看到Robuchon本人滿場跑，</span><span>到處與客人們合照，笑容親切和藹。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>今天吧檯位置不錯，一旁坐的是三二行館老闆。</span></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>中午限定套餐內容如下(NT.3280)：</span><br/><span>---------------</span><br/><span>鴨肝奶酪與南瓜慕斯襯海膽</span></p>
<p><span>荳蔻風味帝王蟹襯酸甜白蘿蔔片</span></p>
<p><span>貝殼麵鑲松茸與朝鮮薊綴阿爾巴白松露</span></p>
<p><span>主餐二選一</span><br/><span>酥煎脆皮日本鯛魚與菠菜及馬拉巴黑椒汁</span><br/><span>or</span><br/><span>香煎澳洲和牛肋眼9+</span></p>
<p><span>肉桂風味翻轉蘋果塔</span></p>
<p><span>咖啡或茶</span><br/><span>---------------</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><span>(菜單)</span></span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9296732951/1383804485-3740410623.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>(吧檯上美麗的擺設)</span></span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9296732951/1383804493-206262484.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>(開放式廚房裡忙翻天)</span></span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9296732951/1383804499-1650097231.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><span>餐前麵包水準依舊，這部分不意外。</span></span></p>
<p><span>南瓜慕斯，海膽與嫩煎鴨肝雖極為類似，皆屬順滑。</span></p>
<p><span>嚐起來口感卻不混淆一起，真難得。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><span>(餐前麵包)</span></span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9296732951/1383804504-159950576.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>(鴨肝奶酪與南瓜慕斯襯海膽)</span></span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9296732951/1383804509-870843288.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>從帝王蟹襯白蘿蔔片與白松露貝殼麵兩道前餐可看出，</span></span></p>
<p><span>Robuchon先生在口感上的創作堪稱瀟灑自如。</span></p>
<p><span>但這一次的松露香氣再度又沉默了，與之前如出一轍，</span></p>
<p><span>似乎被悶住導致香氣無法盡情展。</span></p>
<p><span>大廚剛好站在一旁，但小弟不敢當面詢問他本人，</span></p>
<p><span>決定還是乖乖繼續埋頭享受佳餚。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><span>(荳蔻風味帝王蟹襯酸甜白蘿蔔片)</span></span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9296732951/1383804516-1138542352.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>(貝殼麵鑲松茸與朝鮮薊綴阿爾巴白松露)</span></span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9296732951/1383804527-2737884677.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>(Robuchon大師與我)</span></span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9296732951/1383804520-3611778954.jpg"/><br/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>本日最意外的絕對是牛排，由於在台北吃過不少，</span></span></p>
<p><span>所以刻意點9+澳洲和牛肋眼來比較，</span></p>
<p><span>沒想到好吃得令人想飛上天！</span></p>
<p><span>同樣利用台灣食材做東西融合的比如上次麗禧李主廚，</span></p>
<p><span>但脆脆的龍鬚菜顯然不敵軟嫩的金針菇，</span></p>
<p><span>這菇似乎有細心挑選與處理過，</span></p>
<p><span>味道與牛排有種難以言喻的微妙搭配。</span></p>
<p><span>但這驚艷尚不及肋眼本身的鮮嫩多汁，</span></p>
<p><span>藉高超的手藝及熟度拿捏，讓9+澳洲和牛的油花完整呈現出來，</span></p>
<p><span>與全台北頂尖牛排館平起平坐，佩服之至！</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>(香煎澳洲和牛肋眼9+)</span></span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9296732951/1383804533-1500509458.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>(表現十二分地精彩)</span></span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 12" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9296732951/1383804538-1267060826.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>(主餐之二的酥煎脆皮日本鯛魚與菠菜及馬拉巴黑椒汁)</span></span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 13" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9296732951/1383804543-2875288997.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>(單點的甜點)</span></span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 14" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9296732951/1383804560-571392176.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><span>(肉桂風味翻轉蘋果塔)</span></span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 15" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9296732951/1383804927-2787107771.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><strong>那具有深度的創意卻完全不落俗套，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>以及對於食材口感精準的掌握，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>讓人對於這位大師打從心裡無比地敬仰著。</strong></span></p>
