<p> </p>
<p><span>------------</span><br/><span>蓬萊邨</span><br/><span>地址：台北市大安區仁愛路三段160號B1(福華大飯店)</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2700-2323</span><br/><span>------------</span></p>
<p><span>中午臨時與朋友一同聚餐，地點選在福華大飯店B1的蓬萊邨。</span></p>
<p><span>看名字就知是台菜了，但發文時才發現打不出"邨"這個字。</span></p>
<p><span>原來是村的古體，讀音亦同，</span></p>
<p><span>不過新注音得用ㄊㄨㄣˊ才打得出來。</span></p>
<p><span>(門口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9322485116/1386323026-1626826707.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>在推薦下點了：</span></p>
<p><span>紅悶素菇(NT.280)</span></p>
<p><span>炒山蘇(NT.280)</span></p>
<p><span>清蒸小卷(NT.300)</span></p>
<p><span>軟煎溜肉(NT.300)</span></p>
<p><span>鹽酥豬腳(NT.260)</span></p>
<p><span>紅豆泥芋餅(NT.240)</span></p>
<p><span>起士地瓜泥(NT.80)</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>家住附近，福華是從小吃到大的回憶。</span></p>
<p><span>大概是許久沒來，從一開始的地瓜稀飯就開始感到陌生。</span></p>
<p><span>小菜意外討喜，包括黃金泡菜筍，醃黃瓜，</span><span>甜薑以及漬芥菜心(衝菜)等。</span></p>
<p><span>味道精緻，</span><span>開胃下飯卻一點也不搶戲。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(開胃小菜)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9322485116/1386323032-1345238490.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(漬芥菜心，也就是所謂的衝菜)</span></p>
<p><span> <img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9322485116/1386323037-3203434971.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(無限續的地瓜稀飯)</span></p>
<p><span> <img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9322485116/1386323041-358924112.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span>以台菜來說，蓬萊邨並非讓人印象深刻的突出。</span></p>
<p><span>其中清蒸小卷肉質偏老，</span><span>炒山蘇缺了火候算得上是小失誤吧。</span></p>
<p><span>(清蒸小卷)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9322485116/1386323071-2612459321.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span>(炒山蘇)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9322485116/1386323066-1485663347.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>但軟煎溜肉使用豬里肌，厚切下仍保有極佳嫩度，</span><span>從紅麴得來自然的甜味。</span></p>
<p><span>極少吃到炸豬腳，蓬萊邨先切丁然後油炸，</span><span><strong><span>外皮微酥裡頭保有膠質。</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span>(一下子菜色全上齊)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9322485116/1386323047-3525251422.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p><span>(紅悶素菇)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9322485116/1386323053-1663153575.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(軟煎溜肉)</span></p>
<p><span> <img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9322485116/1386323059-2328627892.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p><span>(鹽酥豬腳)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9322485116/1386323076-74961775.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(外皮微酥裡頭保有膠質)</span></p>
<p><span> <img alt="圖片 12" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9322485116/1386323081-2070839934.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>酥炸芋餅裡包些許紅豆餡，</span><span>有些吃不出所以然來。</span></p>
<p><span>地瓜泥是完成度很高的台式甜點。</span></p>
<p><span>利用焗烤過的起司使甜多了深度，</span></p>
<p><span>藉過篩的動作去地瓜纖維，添上奶油，</span><span>口感如馬鈴薯泥的綿密。</span></p>
<p><span>(花生麻糬)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 13" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9322485116/1386323089-1868797689.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(水果)</span></p>
<p><span> <img alt="圖片 14" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9322485116/1386323094-2629120594.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(起士地瓜泥)</span></p>
<p><span> <img alt="圖片 15" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9322485116/1386323099-2363820047.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(紅豆泥芋餅)</span></p>
