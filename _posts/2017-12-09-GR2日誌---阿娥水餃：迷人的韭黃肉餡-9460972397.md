<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>信任的饕友說了一句：<span><strong>"最愛的韭黃水餃"</strong></span>，</span></p>
<p><span>害我自此念念不忘直到今天終於如願以償。</span></p>
<p><span>店名阿娥，正值下午沒人特地前來。</span></p>

<p><span>(店門口)</span></p>

<p><span>(菜單)</span></p>

<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>用餐內容：</span><br/><span>-----------------</span><br/><span>韭黃蝦仁鮮肉水餃十顆(NT.60)</span><br/><span>酸辣湯(NT.25)</span><br/><span>-----------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>可能是這幾年最合胃口的酸辣湯。</span></p>
<p><span>湯頭不算頂酸，胡椒來得剛好，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>料雖多但與湯頭較有融合一體之感。</strong></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(酸辣湯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9460972397/1512827355-2423264069.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(喜歡這一碗的融合度)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9460972397/1512827363-2831648530.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>水餃包得不算特別細心，有幾顆皮開了。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>內餡口感豐富，不用沾醬就吸引人。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>韭黃香，高麗菜脆，豬肉餡裡的蝦仁帶微彈。</span></p>
<p><span>或許水餃皮不算厲害，那又如何？</span></p>
<p><span>好吃就好了。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
