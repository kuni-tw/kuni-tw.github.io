<p><span>----------------------</span><br/><span>義聚東劉家水餃</span><br/><span>地址：新北市永和區中興街98號</span><br/><span>電話：(02)8921-3000</span><br/><span>時間：11:00~14:00 17:00~21:00</span><br/><span>----------------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>下午臨時餃子癮衝上心頭，</span></p>
<p><span>想也不想飛奔至頂溪站附近的<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/339189245-grd3日誌---義聚東劉家水餃：好吃不過餃子" target="_blank">義聚東</a></strong></span>。</span></p>
<p><span>時值四點五十，店裡雖昏暗，但門已半開。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>外頭板凳上坐幾位客人，原來已經先點好菜了，</span></p>
<p><span>應該是熟客。</span></p>
<p><span>老闆娘聽到我只有一人，先愣了一下，</span></p>
<p><span>但還是微笑放我進來。</span></p>
<p><br/><span>用餐內容：</span><br/><span>----------------------</span><br/><span>酸辣湯(小，NT.40)</span><br/><span>炒青菜(NT.80)</span><br/><span>韭菜豬肉餃十二粒(NT.84)</span><br/><span>----------------------</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(趁五點沒什麼客人進去吃)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9453355517/1476195939-1365130901.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(菜單)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9453355517/1476195948-4182862447.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><br/><span>以前歐洲幾乎每家中國餐廳都只供應酸辣湯，</span></p>
<p><span>喝到怕了，回台後如今這幾年似乎逐漸釋懷。</span></p>
<p><span>今天的酸辣湯也太大碗了，特地跟老闆娘確認一次，</span></p>
<p><span>的確是"小碗"無誤。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(酸辣湯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9453355517/1476195954-4195030058.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(炒青菜)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9453355517/1476195963-423819269.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>一段時間沒吃，幾乎忘記原來義聚東水餃這麼大顆。</span></p>
<p><span>包得極好，內餡飽滿，部分甚至噴汁，嚇了一跳。</span></p>
<p><span>餃皮依舊充滿美味嚼勁，厚實，愈吃愈是欲罷不能。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>我極愛這種肉餡，湯汁與餃皮三者合而為一的境界，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>舒舒服服，不需太多多餘綴飾。</span></p>
<p><span>與名店<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/354634913-%E5%B7%A7%E4%B9%8B%E5%91%B3%E6%89%8B%E5%B7%A5%E6%B0%B4%E9%A4%83" target="_blank">巧之味</a></strong></span>的突出勁道相比，</span></p>
<p><span>義聚東的家常反倒較合我心。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(十二粒水餃已經滿盤)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9453355517/1476195969-859928594.jpg"/> </span></p>

<p><span>(一旁的醬料)</span></p>

<p><span>(內餡飽滿)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9453355517/1476195984-2181264014.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>由於尺寸很大，吃十二顆便飽足，</span></p>
<p><span>不像<strong><span><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/439243111-%E6%B0%B8%E5%92%8C%E8%91%A3%E5%AE%B6%E6%B0%B4%E9%A4%83" target="_blank">董家</a></span></strong>我一口氣能嗑掉四十粒。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>付完帳後冷不防聽一位剛進門的阿嬤大嗓門喊道：</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>"老闆娘，給我十五粒水餃。"</strong></span></p>
<p><span>霎那間感到天旋地轉，淚奔推門衝了出去。</span></p>
