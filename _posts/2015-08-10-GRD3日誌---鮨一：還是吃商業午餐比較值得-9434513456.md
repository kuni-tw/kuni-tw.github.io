<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>----------------------</span><br/><span><strong>鮨一</strong></span><br/><span>地址：台北市中山區民權東路三段60巷4號</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2502-5488</span><br/><span>----------------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>這一天中午吃的是鮨一，位在中山區民權東路巷內。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>板長阿賢來自北市名店笹鮨SASA。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>吃過一次，當時不是很愛：</span><br/><span><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/295003811-grd%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---28092012-%E7%AC%B9%E9%AE%A8-sasa(%E4%B8%8D%E6%8E%A8)" target="_blank">http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/295003811</a></span></p>
<p><span>但優質白身魚及充滿膠質的章魚柔煮至今仍印象深刻。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(鮨一門口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950273-744971539.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(板前)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950275-2128754238.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><br/><span>當然從易親近的商午先試水溫，</span></p>
<p><span>點的是特上套餐(NT.980)</span></p>
<p><span><strong>附生魚片，茶碗蒸，四貫nigiri，散壽司，烤物與味噌。</strong></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>起先的角鰈持平，藉著水果醋讓味覺開始活絡。</span></p>
<p><span>青甘溫度優，微嫩帶點潤口油脂，</span></p>
<p><span>黃鰭鮪赤身也不錯。</span></p>
<p><span>此時試試桌上負責清口的漬蘿蔔，</span></p>
<p><span>可惜有些蘿蔔澀味。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(清口的漬物)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950277-3140889985.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(角鰈)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950279-3024378333.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(來自SASA的板長阿賢)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950281-1516694650.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(青甘)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950284-1674065026.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(鮪魚赤身)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950286-3455434360.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>接下來由茶碗蒸暖活心靈，</span></p>
<p><span>裡頭的蝦子肉質過老，料雖不多，</span></p>
<p><span>但使用的湯頭鮮美，簡單的海菜讓人很有好感。</span></p>
<p><br/><strong><span>首貫鯛魚端的是好彩頭，魚生下的舍利握得鬆，</span></strong></p>
<p><strong><span>重點是入口後整齊逐漸散開。</span></strong></p>
<p><span>這樣精采的表現竟在一般商午給我遇到，令人吃驚。</span></p>
<p><span>接下來的鮭魚亦不惶多讓，</span></p>
<p><span>魚料雖常見，還微炙過，</span></p>
<p><span>但應該是吃過表現最佳的鮭炙燒。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(茶碗蒸)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950288-3927433775.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(開始捏製壽司)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950291-1132659919.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(鯛魚握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950293-3492960460.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(炙燒鮭魚握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 12" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950296-134645892.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><strong>來自俄國的甜蝦又甜又肥又沒異味，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>看來今天狀況極好。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>最後一貫來自台灣的星鰻則可惜，</span></p>
<p><span>阿賢似乎冒險過了頭，錯估醋飯黏性，</span></p>
<p><span>舍利一下子散開，但還是兩個字：<span><strong>"好吃！"</strong></span></span></p>
<p><br/><span>雖然是商午，但散壽司可不馬虎，</span></p>
<p><span>以竹筴魚，漬鮭魚卵與俄羅斯海膽領軍，</span></p>
<p><span>配合表現不錯的醋飯，至此已讓人心滿意足，</span></p>
<p><span>沒想到還有一份中規中矩的烤圓鱈，真是意外。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(俄國甜蝦)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 13" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950299-3942645495.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(台灣星鰻握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 14" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950301-2234224634.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(散壽司)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 15" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950307-1830374689.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(烤圓鱈)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 16" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950309-899765115.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><br/><span>此時其實胃還有空間，加上好奇，</span></p>
<p><span>一問之下沒想到阿賢也有做章魚煮，便單點一份(NT.50)。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>但無法與Sasa的相比，膠質與嫩度皆付之闕如，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>所幸咀嚼中仍帶出一些香氣。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>最後加點聽說今天狀況很優的角鰈鰭邊與牡丹蝦，</span></p>
<p><span>前者沒做常見的炙燒，嫩兼具豐富嚼勁，</span></p>
<p><span>但稍微過油導致膩口。</span></p>
<p><span>來自北海道的牡丹蝦缺了甜度，竟輸給俄國貨，</span></p>
<p><span>讓人失望(NT.260)。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(章魚柔煮)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 17" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950311-697668483.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(角鰈鰭邊握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 18" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950313-1875583199.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(北海道牡丹蝦握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 19" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950316-3150386481.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><br/><span>由於阿賢推薦，又單點一份赤海膽軍艦(NT.320)。</span></p>
<p><span>再度不滿意，過量的醋飯及突兀的海苔香氣，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>讓人深深覺得來這裡還是吃商午比較值得阿。</strong></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(赤海膽軍艦)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 20" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9434513456/1438950318-3178403731.jpg"/> </span></p>
