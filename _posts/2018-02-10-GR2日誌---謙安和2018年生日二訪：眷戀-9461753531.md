<p><span>---------------------</span><br/><span>謙安和</span><br/><span>地址：台北市大安區安和路一段127巷4號</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2700-8128</span><br/><span>---------------------</span></p>
<p><br/><span>生日到來，每年特別找間餐廳慶祝已成慣例。</span></p>
<p><span>去年是Villa Ollila，今年是謙安和，</span></p>
<p><span>想來想去似乎也沒更好的選擇了。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>從西華開始就對和知先生的高超手藝眷戀，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>不論生，熟食皆讓人拜服。</strong></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>晚間扛了朋友從日本幫我帶回的黑龍大吟釀，</span></p>
<p><span>1.8公升的超大瓶裝應該夠喝。</span></p>
<p><span>和知與往常一般幽默，帶著拿捏極好的分寸，</span></p>
<p><span>不給人絲毫做作之感。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(筷架與光線)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274299-3053364078.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(和知料理長)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274302-2107296321.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(選吟釀杯，開瓶費一人NT.200)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274306-60125134.jpg"/> </span></p>

<p><span>(黑龍1.8公升)</span></p>

<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>首先三品。</span></p>
<p><span>炸河豚白子細緻，熱燙恰好。</span></p>
<p><span>安康魚肝溫得巧，醬汁甜美。</span></p>
<p><span>諸子魚難得吃到，烤得香酥。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(炸河豚白子，安康魚肝，諸子魚)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274313-2292952915.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(諸子魚)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274316-1654565920.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>接下來風呂大根，上頭滿溢雞肉末，溫暖讓人回神。</span></p>
<p><span>一邊觀賞料理長那一絲不苟的料理手法與精神。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>我喜歡態度嚴謹而非過度自信的料理人，掌控食材而非被食材掌控。</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>台灣這樣的日料板前不多，但若願意仔細走訪，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>偶爾還是能遇著一兩位。</strong></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(風呂大根)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274319-1830663425.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>今晚生魚片或許不夠美，但刀工，溫度無可挑剔。</span></p>
<p><span>魚生厚度剛好，嚼勁剛好，醬汁剛好。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>白蘿蔔絲不知怎麼切的，何等清爽。</strong></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(生魚片沾醬)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274322-1206989105.jpg"/> </span></p>

<p><span>(造身)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274324-3117350115.jpg"/> </span></p>

<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>椀物是和知最擅長，畢竟師承神田。</span></p>
<p><span>或許在湯頭呈現上已難有更高一層的進展，</span></p>
<p><span>但丸子太妙，<span><strong>豆皮與魚漿的結合難以置信。</strong></span></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(椀物)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 14" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274338-3102245001.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(豆皮丸子真棒)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 15" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274341-1119652963.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>原本電話中有告知希望套餐裡能有壽司，</span></p>
<p><span>但仍被打槍，因為謙安和中午生晚間熟是原則。</span></p>
<p><span>忽然間看到和知開始片魚馬上感到驚喜：</span></p>
<p><span>本鮪中腹甜美而爽快，漬本鮪赤身看似霜燙過，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>一問之下更令人費解。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>和知說先將赤身浸熱水稍微燙過再醃漬一天。</span></p>
<p><span>"先霜燙後醃漬，醃漬一天？"</span></p>
<p><span>"是的，但配方不同，不只醬油，酒，還有別的。"</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(和知開始捏壽司)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 16" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274344-3332097344.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(本鮪中腹)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 17" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274346-2104919743.jpg"/> </span></p>

<p><span><img alt="圖片 18" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274350-3224726614.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>太刀偏弱，熟度沒問題，但熱度與脆度不足。</span></p>
<p><span>酒粕鴨肝扳回一城，香氣迷人，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>但似乎不完全來自上頭現刨的松露，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>因為還沒刨之前我就遠遠聞到另一股香氣，</span></p>
<p><span>可惜酒酣耳熱之際忘了仔細觀察。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(烤太刀)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 19" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274353-3506733148.jpg"/> </span></p>

<p> </p>
<p><span>鱈魚白子上頭撒滿牛蒡絲與青海苔，</span></p>
<p><span>美得難以置信，可惜似乎煎過頭了。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(鱈魚白子)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 21" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274360-3213766735.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(真美)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 22" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274363-2519675056.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>趁著台灣開放日本和牛進口，謙安和不免跟風。</span></p>
<p><span>但與之前使用澳和相比，今晚的日和顯然更高明。</span></p>
<p><span>炸過的和牛入口瞬間有噴汁錯覺，心都要融化。</span></p>
<p><span>"來自宮崎縣的和牛"</span></p>
<p><span>"A5嗎？"</span></p>
<p><span>"不，A4而已。"</span></p>
<p><span><strong>並非使用最高級的A5，但我被味道說服了。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>入口那迅速化開的甜美油脂幾乎已達飽和，</span></p>
<p><span>那一撮山葵適時在尾段解膩也是微妙，</span></p>
<p><span>整體來說，就像一股美味幾秒鐘後轉為甘泉，</span></p>
<p><span>實在欣賞這種互補的平衡。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(炸日本和牛)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 23" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9461753531/1518274367-1549344318.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(美味！)</span></p>

<p> </p>
<p><span>和知的丼飯是不可不嚐的亮點之一。</span></p>
<p><span>在我心中只有星Hoshi料亭曾經更勝一籌過，</span></p>
<p><span>但雙方差異在伯仲之間。</span></p>
<p><span>可惜星Hoshi後來歇業，如今已成絕響。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>今天是海膽毛蟹丼飯，名字充滿罪惡感入口卻是溫暖。</strong></span></p>
