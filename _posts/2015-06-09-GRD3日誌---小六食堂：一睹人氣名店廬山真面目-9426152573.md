<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>---------</span><br/><span>小六食堂</span><br/><span>地址：台北市中山區松江路259巷8號</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2506-0065</span><br/><span>時間：11:30~14:00 18:00~21:00</span><br/><span>---------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>小六食堂，朋友間流傳許久的人氣名店。</span></p>
<p><span>試打電話過多次卻無功而返，超級難訂到想罵三字經。</span></p>
<p><span>五月終於時來運轉，跟到晚餐團，</span></p>
<p><span>一人八百元的無菜單料理。</span></p>
<p><span>晚間抵達，店內明亮，客人如預期完全滿座。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(人氣名店小六食堂)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426152573/1433862997-303775559.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>坐定後開始上餐，</span><span>白玉蛤蠣與味噌花椰負責打頭陣。</span></p>
<p><span>前者比預期來得肥大，幾乎把殼快給撐破了。</span></p>
<p><span>後者煮得很透，</span><span>淡薄的味道藉著調好的味噌醬而豐富了。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>接下來的烤鮑魚嫩到一個極致，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>甚至有噴汁的錯覺，真是相當不容易。</strong></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(酒蒸蛤蜊)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426152573/1433780394-881132797.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(味噌花椰)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426152573/1433780397-2868286615.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(烤鮑魚)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426152573/1433780400-3116940330.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><br/><span>第一盤綜合生魚片讓人開心，</span></p>
<p><span>新鮮度與溫度剛好，蝦子夠黏與甜。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>魚生切工雖少了美感，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>但下刀俐落，加上切得厚實，所以過癮。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>第二盤則是黑鮪，除赤身外還有皮油部位，</span><span>依舊爽快過人。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(第一盤是綜合生魚片)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426152573/1433780402-1965012751.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(大干貝與鮭魚肚)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426152573/1433780405-2978107371.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(第二盤是台灣黑鮪)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426152573/1433780407-2133176298.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>烤金時鯛外表鋪滿粗鹽，看似不起眼，</span><span>掀開皮後大有文章：</span></p>
<p><span>微微帶著膠質與juicy，真要冠以鮮美來形容。</span></p>
<p><span>加上刺少，這價位能吃到這等級的烤物真棒。</span></p>
<p><span>蒜味嗆蝦母的確夠嗆，</span><span>但沒下一盤劍旗魚來得耀眼：</span></p>
<p><span>看似乾澀的外表裡頭半生的巧妙熟度，</span><span>與柚子胡椒及美乃滋搭配很優。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(鹽烤金時鯛)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426152573/1433780409-4146173564.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(蒜味嗆蝦母)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426152573/1433780412-1349300616.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(劍旗魚)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426152573/1433780415-3382587699.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><br/><span>雞肉丸子不愧小樹師傅招牌菜之一，</span><span>柔軟異常在咀嚼中令人印象深刻。</span></p>
<p><span>明太子綠竹筍也讚，</span><span>筍子鮮甜藉微辣與香氣適度引出來。</span></p>
<p><span>黑鮪鮭魚卵丼飯看似平凡，卻不馬虎。</span></p>
<p><span>鮪魚泥打得極細，鮭魚卵漬得巧，鹹中帶甜。</span></p>
<p><span>仔細挖了一口，真是滿足，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>這才發現原來小六有提供醋飯，但卻沒有握壽司。</strong></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(雞肉丸子) </span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 12" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426152573/1433780418-3655496244.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(明太子綠竹筍)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 13" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426152573/1433780421-1764240619.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(黑鮪鮭魚卵丼)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 14" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426152573/1433780424-2880187896.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>小六生意這麼好，並不是沒有道理的，</span><span>連負責收尾的味噌湯也細心。</span></p>
<p><span>裡頭魚肉切薄塊，外型工整。</span></p>
<p><strong><span>最重要的是，"盡可能避開魚骨魚刺"，</span></strong></p>
<p><span>不像時下許多平價料亭</span><span>直接將切剩魚塊扔湯鍋一起煮。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(味噌湯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 15" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426152573/1433780427-1692394234.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><strong>有機會一定要來試試中午的海鮮丼。</strong></span></p>
