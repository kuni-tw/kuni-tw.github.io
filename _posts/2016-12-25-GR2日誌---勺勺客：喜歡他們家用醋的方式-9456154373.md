<p> </p>
<p><span>好友看完我文章後大力推薦勺勺客的水餃。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>這傢伙平常東挑西挑，對中菜更是愛憎分明，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>難得一句好話實在讓我無法抗拒，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>中午便特地前來一嚐究竟。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(門口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9456154373/1482577922-3615445970.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(裝潢)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9456154373/1482577930-4278389753.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>用餐內容：</span><br/><span>------------------</span><br/><span>新疆香酥羊肉串(NT.160)</span><br/><span>金邊娃娃菜(NT.180)</span><br/><span>一兩元寶(NT.50)</span><br/><span>酸嗆土豆絲(NT.140)</span><br/><span>燒羊肉鮮湯(NT.180)</span><br/><span>------------------</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(招待的毛豆)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9456154373/1482577938-616607142.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(先將泡饃用的麵餅交給客人撕)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9456154373/1482577946-448135157.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(店家說撕成玉米粒大小，但個人建議再大些)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9456154373/1482577954-2230339775.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><strong>首盤羊肉串便是驚喜的開始，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>酥脆，愈嚼愈是無法罷口。</span></p>
<p><span>餃子就是喜愛的皮餡合一境界，</span></p>
<p><span>韭菜味突出，不過這樣算下來一顆十塊，</span></p>
<p><span>是近期內吃過最貴的水餃。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(新疆香酥羊肉串)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9456154373/1482577961-1665709989.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(餃子取名"一兩元寶")</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9456154373/1482577975-31349781.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>土豆絲令人讚賞。</span></p>
<p><span>熱，細切工整，比<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/445354634-gr2%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---%E5%BE%90%E6%B7%AE%E4%BA%BA%E5%AE%B6%EF%BC%9A%E5%96%9C%E6%84%9B%E5%A4%A7%E6%A1%8C%E8%8F%9C%E7%9A%84%E6%A8%82%E8%B6%A3" target="_blank">徐淮人家</a></strong></span>更酸溜。</span></p>
<p><span>難以想像這看似簡單的一道菜如此令人心儀，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>入口後醋香衝腦實在美妙。</strong></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(個人很喜愛的一道：酸嗆土豆絲)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9456154373/1482577984-3792019741.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>娃娃菜依舊以醋酸為主，雖油不膩。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>至此我發現我非常喜歡勺勺客用醋的方式，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>不過老闆說，其實他這幾年已不斷將酸度減弱，</span></p>
<p><span>不然原本陝西調味風格酸到連牙齒都受不了。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(金邊娃娃菜)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9456154373/1482577968-2725216705.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>最後的泡饃不算對味，羊肉過柴是主因。</span></p>
<p><span>但湯頭層次與討喜，微酸自然順口。</span></p>
<p><span>所謂的泡饃指的就是一種未經發酵及烤製的麵餅，</span></p>
<p><span>食用前先用手將之撕成小塊狀然後扔入肉湯滾煮，</span></p>
<p><span>口感有些類似麵疙瘩。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(泡饃名曰"燒羊肉鮮湯")</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9456154373/1482577993-1010910581.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(剛剛撕好的麵餅碎塊)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 12" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9456154373/1482578000-2673426953.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>臨走前看到隔壁桌甜點極為吸睛，</span></p>
<p><span>一問之下才知得事先預訂，當天點肯定沒得吃。</span></p>
<p><span>步出店門已開始思索再訪之期。</span></p>
