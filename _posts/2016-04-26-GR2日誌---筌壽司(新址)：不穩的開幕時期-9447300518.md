<p> </p>
<p><span>------------</span><br/><span>筌壽司</span><br/><span>地址：台北市大安區安和路一段78巷37號</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2700-8779</span><br/><span>時間：12:00~14:30 18:00~22:00</span><br/><span>------------</span><span><br/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><strong><a href="https://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/294997760-grd%e6%97%a5%e8%aa%8c---%e7%ad%8c%e5%a3%bd%e5%8f%b82009%e5%b9%b4%e9%a6%96%e8%a8%aa%ef%bc%9a%e4%b8%8d%e6%8e%a8%ef%bc%81" target="_blank">筌壽司多年前吃過一次</a>，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>當時還是安東街上一家簡樸日式料亭，網路上知名。</span></p>
<p><span>可惜阿筌板長那天失誤連連，所以再也沒踏進。</span></p>
<p><span>一段時間後聽說筌停止營業，這一歇便是好幾年。</span></p>
<p><span>2016阿筌師傅找到了新店面，重起爐灶，</span><span>立時撥電話過去訂位。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>中午來到米販食堂斜對面，筌壽司裝潢討喜，</span></p>
<p><span>簡潔大方的鐵門，不會有拒人於千里之外之感。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>可惜外場在訂位上出差錯，讓人當場幾乎發火。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>不過既來之則安之，坐定後不囉嗦，請師傅直接開始。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(門口)</span></p>

<p><span>(小徑)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658000-1146454740.jpg"/> </span></p>

<p><span>(牆上的證書)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658020-259933446.jpg"/></span></p>

<p><span>(水族箱的毛蟹)</span></p>
<p><span> <img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658048-2423727413.jpg"/> </span></p>

<p><span>(雄糾糾氣昂昂的毛蟹)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658074-3890377139.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>選的是NT.1500的商午握壽司套餐，</span></p>
<p><span>外場毛豆忘了給，<span><strong><a href="https://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/294997760-grd%e6%97%a5%e8%aa%8c---%e7%ad%8c%e5%a3%bd%e5%8f%b82009%e5%b9%b4%e9%a6%96%e8%a8%aa%ef%bc%9a%e4%b8%8d%e6%8e%a8%ef%bc%81" target="_blank">想起首訪的負面回憶。</a></strong></span></span></p>
<p><span>開胃小缽是無味的起手式，接下來便是冗長的等待。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(毛豆)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658115-3118726531.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(開胃小缽)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658135-71367349.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>首貫握壽司是真鯛，強烈的紅醋飯及尾韻的紫蘇，</span><span>有著單純的微甜。</span></p>
<p><span>友人若有所思："你不覺得壽司距離好遠嗎？"</span></p>
<p><span>仔細一瞧，用餐檯面果然比尋常來得大許多。</span></p>
<p><span>本人雙手伸直超過180cm，仍無法輕易拿到檯上的壽司，</span><span>除非前傾上半身。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(多年不見的阿筌師傅)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658158-2603934495.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(真鯛握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658185-467898322.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>第二貫是海膽，型有些過於隨意與鬆散，</span><span>在倒下前趕緊用筷子救回。</span></p>
<p><span>白甘持平，醋飯味道依舊強烈，雖仍不至搶戲，</span><span>但開始有些擔心。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(海膽握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658203-4193487367.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(白甘握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 12" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658221-3256833722.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>接下來是北海道干貝與星鰻，繼續持平與濃烈高張力。</span></p>
<p><strong><span>終於到了甜蝦，醋飯味道首度蓋過上頭蝦子的甜美，</span></strong><span>擔心果然成真。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(干貝握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 13" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658237-1168937604.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(阿筌師傅遞來星鰻)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 14" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658262-3484252815.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(星鰻握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 15" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658280-680146339.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(可憐的甜蝦)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 16" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658296-3833465376.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>茶碗蒸來的正是時候，銀杏與山藥適度讓心情回暖。</span></p>
<p><span>下一貫是個人鍾愛的漬鮪魚赤身，</span></p>
<p><span>阿筌師傅將魚生汆燙過，微浸醬油，<span><strong>但醃漬程度不足，</strong></span></span><span>可惜。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(茶碗蒸)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 17" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658321-2025702174.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(漬鮪魚赤身)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 18" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658340-384363559.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>一旁觀察板前二手，動作極度生澀外，</span></p>
<p><span>忽然間切好細捲便擱在料理檯上，改做其他客人丼飯。</span></p>
<p><span>眼看二手忙進忙出，<span><strong>那細捲獨自依偎在那楚楚可憐。</strong></span></span></p>
<p><span>心想那該不會是我們的細捲吧，</span></p>
<p><span>結果真的，二手隔很久後才把料理檯上那<span><strong>"風乾過"</strong></span>的細捲端來。</span></p>
<p><span>此時阿筌師傅同時遞來我的小肌，這順序？</span></p>
<p><span>"風乾過"的細捲有兩種，各吃一塊後就不想碰了。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(獨自被擱在料理上良久的細捲)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 19" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658360-50395247.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(兩種細捲)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 20" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658379-3572955893.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>小肌味道與醋飯完全不合，簡直是悲劇。</span></p>
<p><span>鮭魚卵軍艦隨即上桌，</span><span>整體過鹹算是小缺憾。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(小肌握)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 21" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658406-3587276541.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(漬鮭魚卵軍艦)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 22" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658427-137827698.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>料亭裡的音樂讓人印象深刻，</span><span>是小提琴名曲流浪者之歌。</span></p>
<p><span>也是周星馳鍾愛的曲目，</span><span>曾在"功夫"裡用上整首曲子的不同段落來配樂。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(味噌湯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 23" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658452-815868771.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(甜點是麵茶慕斯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 24" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9447300518/1461658477-2946641912.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>嚴格說來，阿筌師傅比多年前強許多，</span></p>
<p><span>其中食材的鮮度以及握功皆進化不少。</span></p>
<p><span>走出店外時，心想著，</span><span><strong>或許晚餐的Omakase混用紅白醋飯會比商午表現來得好吧。</strong></span></p>
