<p><span>-------------------------------------</span><br/><span>Sunny Kitchen</span><br/><span>地址：台北市大安區四維路214號</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2314-5333</span><br/><span>-------------------------------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><strong>美麗的老闆娘Sunny朝餐飲界又繼續邁進一步，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>繼去年Sunny Cafe後，</span></p>
<p><span>今年六月六號又開了Sunny Kitchen。</span></p>
<p><span>這一次直接進軍高級餐廳雲集的大安區四維路，</span></p>
<p><span>胃口不小！</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>中午到場，以淺色為主的裝潢馬上帶來好感。</span></p>
<p><span>透過落地窗得到大方與宜人的太陽光。</span></p>
<p><span>菜單上見到義式燉飯就沒辦法啦，</span></p>
<p><span>非點一份試試：</span></p>
<p><span>牛肝菌野菇奶油燉飯(NT.210)</span></p>
<p><span>加NT.100升級為套餐，</span></p>
<p><span>附湯，麵包，沙拉與飲料。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(門口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426006845/1433582346-429306671.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(中午光線宜人)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426006845/1433582349-3931829765.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(店內)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426006845/1433582354-3355628246.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>可惜上餐順序出了差錯，</span></p>
<p><span>大概是剛開幕還不穩的關係吧。</span></p>
<p><span>外場先端來Risotto，然後隔一段時間麵包與湯，</span></p>
<p><span>最後才給沙拉，呃...</span></p>
<p><br/><span><strong>麵包鬆軟，蘑菇濃湯味道簡單與單一，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>沙拉在類似Balsamic酒醋做基底的醬汁引領下，</span></p>
<p><span>逐漸將我情緒拉回。</span></p>
<p><span>整體而言，這價位的附餐很okay。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(牛肝菌義式燉飯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426006845/1433582356-4142500977.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(本日湯，沙拉與麵包)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426006845/1433582358-2399676759.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><strong>燉飯比預期優許多，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>最難以掌握的熟度竟相當巧妙給避開了要害。</span></p>
<p><span>透至只剩下一丁點的米心，</span></p>
<p><span>這樣的熟度風格與文華Bencotto有些接近。</span></p>
<p><span>當然對少數人而言可能仍過熟，</span></p>
<p><span>但一樣也會有人覺得偏生。</span></p>
<p><br/><span><strong>醬汁收得確實，在米粒上縮成有光澤的薄膜。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>濃郁中冒出極易辨識的牛肝菌香氣，</span></p>
<p><span>但與似乎過頭的鮮奶油無法處在一個平衡，</span></p>
<p><span>咀嚼之中會逐漸膩口，有點可惜。</span></p>
<p><br/><span>好奇下詢問今天統管內場的呂主廚，</span></p>
<p><span>原來是使用Gallo的Arborio，</span></p>
<p><span>乃義大利三大Risotto米種之一。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>成本不低，但將這樣一盤義式燉飯壓在NT.210，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>真是非常難得。</strong></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(醬汁收得確實) </span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426006845/1433582361-886438845.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(美麗的老闆娘Sunny看起來好開心啊！)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9426006845/1433582365-2514153203.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>瞥見菜單內還有NT.720的Prime級肋眼，</span></p>
<p><span>相當吸引人的價位，</span></p>
<p><span>有機會一定會來試試的啊。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
