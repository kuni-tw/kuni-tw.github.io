<div class="article-content-inner" id="article-content-inner"><p><span><img alt="圖片 1" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9428003342/1436465211-1244449087.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(經典的下港排骨酥湯)</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>------------------------------------</span><br/><span>下港米糕排骨酥</span><br/><span>時間：10:30~23:00</span><br/><span>地址：新北市永和區保平路14號</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2232-5739</span><br/><span>------------------------------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><strong>表演前照例會減重兩三天。</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>當天教完課後餓到受不了啦，忽然想念起下港的排骨酥湯，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>臨時便跑到永和保平路。</strong></span></p>
<p><br/><span>炎熱午後走進與多年前全然不同的嶄新裝潢裡，</span></p>
<p><span>添購了電動門，冷氣以及舒適。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>一共點了：</span><br/><span>----------------------</span><br/><span>筒仔米糕(NT.30)</span><br/><span>魯白菜(小，NT.15)</span><br/><span>魯筍絲(小，NT.15)</span><br/><span>嘴邊瘦肉(NT.50)</span><br/><span>魯肉飯(小，NT.25)</span><br/><span>排骨酥湯(NT.45)</span><br/><span>----------------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(門口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9428003342/1436465070-2944995832.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(菜單)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9428003342/1436465073-305594063.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/294999374-grd%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---19052010-%E4%B8%8B%E6%B8%AF%E7%B1%B3%E7%B3%95%E6%8E%92%E9%AA%A8%E9%85%A5" target="_blank">2010年之前常來的下港</a>，如今味道已些許不同。</span></p>
<p><span>主要是米糕，怎會變成這個樣子呢？</span></p>
<p><span>香氣付之闕如外，</span></p>
<p><span>米飯蒸得過乾柴且調味平庸，讓人大失所望。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>常吃嘴邊肉，只要去台式小吃多會點上一盤，</span></p>
<p><span>除少數例子外大多口感偏韌。</span></p>
<p><span>下港亦如此，但所幸還有非常對味的沾醬，</span></p>
<p><span>微辣中帶點深度與開胃。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(菜色上齊)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9428003342/1436465091-1043032975.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(筍絲，滷白菜與筒仔米糕)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9428003342/1436465124-2793402642.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>魯肉飯可惜了，飯煮得過乾硬。</span></p>
<p><span>濃稠間帶著些許膠質的滷汁還算有個性，</span></p>
<p><span>可惜愈吃愈是膩口。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><br/><span>排骨酥湯依舊突出，尤其這幾年吃過多家之後，</span></p>
<p><span>對它更是另眼相看。</span></p>
<p><span>好比魯肉飯名店金峰也有賣：<a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/295005593-grd3%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---26042013-%E5%B0%8B%E8%A8%AA%E4%BA%94%E5%A4%A7%E9%AD%AF%E8%82%89%E9%A3%AF%E5%85%B6%E5%9B%9B%EF%BC%9A%E9%87%91%E5%B3%B0" target="_blank">http://goo.gl/qtYd8d</a></span></p>
<p><span>俐落冬瓜香，但味道太過乾淨，缺了癮頭。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>下港的排骨肉留得多，極入味與軟爛，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>湯頭非常濃郁，藉著適當的胡椒與熱度暖活心靈，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>然後引出複雜且充滿層次的甜美，太爽快了。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>唯一能與之相抗衡的大概只在矮仔財嚐過：<a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/300177884-grd3%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---22102013-%E8%A2%AB%E5%95%86%E5%91%A8%E8%AD%BD%E7%82%BA%E5%85%A8%E5%8F%B0%E7%81%A3%E7%AC%AC%E4%B8%80%E5%90%8D%E7%9A%84" target="_blank">http://goo.gl/uv6jh4</a></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(魯肉飯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9428003342/1436465171-1113106007.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(經典的排骨酥湯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9428003342/1436465211-1244449087.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><strong>付完帳後心情還不錯，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>晚間的演出相當順利，大功告成，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>實在很愛這種經典的台味啊。</strong></span></p>
