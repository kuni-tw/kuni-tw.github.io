<p><span>(新來來的韭菜與韭黃水餃)</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>-------------------</span><br/><span>新來來水餃川菜館</span><br/><span>地址：台北市萬華區青年路46號</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2301-4528</span><br/><span>時間：11:00~13:45 17:00~20:45</span><br/><span>-------------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>難得又遇到一家心儀的水餃。</span></p>
<p><span>在此前最愛一直都是位於永和的義聚東，</span></p>
<p><span>就算後來陸續吃了幾家名店，排行仍從未撼動。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>本日用餐內容：</span><br/><span>-------------------</span><br/><span>韭黃水餃(十粒NT.70)</span><br/><span>韭菜水餃(十粒NT.70)</span><br/><span>酸辣湯(中份NT.70)</span><br/><span>麻辣牛肚絲(NT.220)</span><br/><span>炒高麗菜(小份NT.100)</span><br/><span>水煮牛肉(小份NT.250)</span><br/><span>-------------------</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(新來來)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9463323164/1529189160-1973283054.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(門口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9463323164/1529189162-4083640109.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(菜單)</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9463323164/1529189164-3100882234.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>新來來名曰川菜館，但實際上品嚐毫無川菜之感。</span></p>
<p><span>酸辣湯味道平淡，牛肚絲微麻微辣，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>而水煮牛肉恐怕是至今吃過最離奇的，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>底下還學台式小火鍋放一盞酒精燈負責保溫。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(酸辣湯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9463323164/1529189167-3590471924.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(麻辣牛肚絲)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9463323164/1529189170-3714895017.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(單看照片很難猜得出是什麼的水煮牛肉)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9463323164/1529189181-4080660267.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(炒高麗菜)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9463323164/1529189184-3977312783.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><strong>但水餃真的是好，一顆接一顆，沾點醋或醬油。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>外皮微夾生，入口嚼勁，尤其愛它們皮與餡的緊密契合。</span></p>
<p><span>尺寸不算大，單價一顆七塊台幣亦不算便宜，</span></p>
<p><span>但整體質感極佳，當中尤其韭黃內餡深得我心，</span></p>
<p><span>猶勝中山區南京東路以韭黃水餃知名的阿娥。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(新來來的水餃)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9463323164/1529189173-4272283489.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(韭菜內餡)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 10" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9463323164/1529189175-2011688931.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(韭黃內餡)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 11" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9463323164/1529189178-3906674098.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><strong>離場前呆望著帳單，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>心想以後來這裡還是單純吃水餃就好了。</strong></span></p>
