<p><span>前<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/447107708-gr2%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---%E5%A1%9E%E5%AD%90%E5%B0%8F%E9%85%92%E9%A4%A8%EF%BC%9A%E9%80%99%E5%B9%BE%E5%B9%B4%E4%BE%86%E6%9C%80%E5%84%AA%E8%B3%AA%E7%9A%84%E5%8F%B0%E7%81%A3" target="_blank">塞子老闆Raven</a></strong></span>選在永和重起爐灶，</span></p>
<p><span>新餐酒館名Reel，</span><span>18號開幕當日與好友們準時抵達。</span></p>
<p><span>比塞子更寬闊明亮的空間，木造裝潢與熟悉的笑容。</span></p>
<p><span>但外場依舊只有一位，依舊忙進忙出，</span></p>
<p><span>不知有沒有辦法負荷得了周末晚間的熱絡。</span></p>

<p><span>用餐內容：</span><br/><span>---------</span><br/><span>前菜</span><br/><span>勃根地紅酒醬煮水波蛋(NT.240)</span><br/><span>鮪魚醬東寶黑豬里肌(NT.320)</span><br/><span>巴斯克燉菜襯拜庸火腿(NT.340)</span><br/><span>主餐</span><br/><span>中東羊肉串佐黎麥優格(NT.520)</span><br/><span>葡式香烤非洲雞腿(NT.560)</span><br/><span>US紐約客牛排佐綠胡椒芥末醬時蔬(NT.660)</span><br/><span>---------</span></p>
<p><span>與<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/447107708-gr2%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---%E5%A1%9E%E5%AD%90%E5%B0%8F%E9%85%92%E9%A4%A8%EF%BC%9A%E9%80%99%E5%B9%BE%E5%B9%B4%E4%BE%86%E6%9C%80%E5%84%AA%E8%B3%AA%E7%9A%84%E5%8F%B0%E7%81%A3">塞子時期</a></strong></span>最大差別之一就是點主餐附沙拉與湯。</span></p>
<p><span>應該是為配合中永和區的價位水平，</span></p>
<p><span>沙拉是附餐，毫不馬虎，</span></p>
<p><span>無亮麗擺盤，但新鮮與脆口的菜葉。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>充滿企圖心的軟嫩鴨胗，有著豪邁的尺寸。</strong></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>雖然只是玉米濃湯，但注入巧思，</span></p>
<p><span>湯裡充斥微炙過的玉米粒，偶然浮出松露味。</span></p>
<p><span>(附餐沙拉)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="/assets/images/9448999115/1466334475-1841865666.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>(附湯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="/assets/images/9448999115/1466334563-2917211286.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>今天的整體菜色相較於<strong><span><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/447107708-gr2%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---%E5%A1%9E%E5%AD%90%E5%B0%8F%E9%85%92%E9%A4%A8%EF%BC%9A%E9%80%99%E5%B9%BE%E5%B9%B4%E4%BE%86%E6%9C%80%E5%84%AA%E8%B3%AA%E7%9A%84%E5%8F%B0%E7%81%A3" target="_blank">塞子時期</a></span></strong>淡味許多。</span></p>
<p><span>有時少了關鍵的亮點，或令人期待的細心。</span></p>
<p><span>好比拜庸火腿熱燙與燉菜一齊端出，</span></p>
<p><span>味道不差，</span><span>但沒將這款火腿的氣質適當襯托出來。</span></p>
<p><span>鮪魚醬豬里肌或許再切薄些，能去除一點偏韌。</span></p>
<p><span>(勃根地紅酒醬煮水波蛋)</span></p>

<p><span>主餐亦缺乏<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/447107708-gr2%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---%E5%A1%9E%E5%AD%90%E5%B0%8F%E9%85%92%E9%A4%A8%EF%BC%9A%E9%80%99%E5%B9%BE%E5%B9%B4%E4%BE%86%E6%9C%80%E5%84%AA%E8%B3%AA%E7%9A%84%E5%8F%B0%E7%81%A3" target="_blank">塞子時期</a></strong></span>的強烈個性。</span></p>
<p><span>羊肉串香料用足，端的是異國風情。</span></p>
<p><span>雞腿入口溫和微辣，偷走了驚奇。</span></p>
<p><span>牛排過度薄切，不過起碼維持住一點粗獷，</span></p>
<p><span>與水準之上的熟度。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>三道皆無以往讓人想找酒來喝的衝動，真是意外。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>(中東羊肉串佐黎麥優格)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 15" src="/assets/images/9448999115/1466334579-3634672912.jpg"/></span></p>

<p><span><img alt="圖片 17" src="/assets/images/9448999115/1466334604-3761639177.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span>此行雖然不算滿意，尤其是與前身<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/447107708-gr2%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---%E5%A1%9E%E5%AD%90%E5%B0%8F%E9%85%92%E9%A4%A8%EF%BC%9A%E9%80%99%E5%B9%BE%E5%B9%B4%E4%BE%86%E6%9C%80%E5%84%AA%E8%B3%AA%E7%9A%84%E5%8F%B0%E7%81%A3" target="_blank">塞子</a></strong></span>相比。</span></p>
<p><span>但很高興中永和繼<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/446499800-gr2%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%8C---roy's-food-lab-%E7%BE%85%E7%BE%A9%E7%94%9F%E6%B4%BB%EF%BC%9A%E7%B2%BE%E5%BD%A9%E7%9A%84%E7%BE%8A%E6%8E%92%EF%BC%81" target="_blank">Roy's</a></strong></span>之後又一家高水準餐館進駐。</span></p>
<p><span>向來討厭一成不變，唾棄過度保守的懶惰chef。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>尤其當傲慢成為一種無可救藥的失職藉口時，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>即是廚藝者沉淪於平庸的起頭。</strong></span></p>
