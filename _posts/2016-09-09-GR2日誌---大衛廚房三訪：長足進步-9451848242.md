<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>大衛廚房陳老闆企圖心足，</span></p>
<p><span><span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/363146651-%E5%A4%A7%E8%A1%9B%E5%BB%9A%E6%88%BF-david's-pasta" target="_blank">2014年首訪</a></strong></span>不滿意後鍥而不捨繼續寫信來邀約。</span></p>
<p><span>可惜再訪狀況依舊，當然也可能是調整過了頭，</span></p>
<p><span>好比白醬的比例。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(大衛廚房)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9451848242/1473411293-934386653.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>這一次老闆應該是有備而來，</span></p>
<p><span>看到信裡提及咖哩很難克制我早按捺不住的好奇心。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>用餐內容：</span><br/><span>-----------------------</span><br/><span>蒜苗洋芋鮮蚵湯(NT.50)</span><br/><span>炸蚵仔(NT.100)</span><br/><span>咖哩豬排飯(NT.155+歐姆蛋NT.50)</span><br/><span>Prime肋眼(NT.800)</span><br/><span>奶油白醬海鮮麵(NT.230+辣椒NT.20)</span><br/><span>-----------------------</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span><strong>今天餐點有長足進步。</strong></span></p>
<p><span>本日濃湯以馬鈴薯打成泥，蚵仔湯頭突出，</span></p>
<p><span>偶爾浮出九層塔，鮮美，<span><strong>可惜味道微雜亂。</strong></span></span></p>
<p><span>炸蚵一盤不論尺寸份量都很大，炸衣尚可，</span></p>
<p><span>但這價位很okay。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(本日的洋芋鮮蚵湯與大蒜麵包) </span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9451848242/1473411300-2029100082.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(炸蚵仔)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9451848242/1473411307-4099189330.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>意外的是咖哩豬排飯。</span></p>
<p><span>豬排肉質雖不夠細緻，但鬆軟藏肉汁。</span></p>
<p><span>來自胡椒鹽的鹹度討喜，可惜與蚵仔一樣有炸衣缺憾。</span></p>
<p><span>淋飯上的咖哩醬汁已有相當高完成度，</span></p>
<p><span><strong>雖吃得出用咖哩塊調製，但與其他材料配合良好，</strong></span></p>
<p><span>連洋蔥也炒至焦化，算是喜愛的日式咖哩風格。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(日式炸豬排) </span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9451848242/1473411313-364804753.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(咖哩蛋包飯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9451848242/1473411320-2756954476.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(豬排斷面)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9451848242/1473411326-1457537327.jpg"/></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>牛排有些犯規了。</span></p>
<p><span>菜單上寫14oz的Prime級肋眼，NT.800，</span></p>
<p><span>但上桌時比預期小，老闆說因怕我吃不下所以切8oz。</span></p>
<p><span>試吃後當下便明白，厚實與炙燒香足，</span></p>
<p><span>端的是Prime美牛肉質，油花分布均勻，幾乎沒什麼筋，</span></p>
<p><span>看來都給老闆事先剔除了，難怪只有8oz。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(8oz的Prime美牛肋眼)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9451848242/1473411334-2432783630.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(油花還算均勻)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9451848242/1473411342-196725689.jpg"/></span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>台式白醬吃多了，聽到有辣椒版當然要嚐嚐。</span></p>
<p><span>類似咖哩紅醬色澤入口後卻是椒辣突出的奶油白醬，</span></p>
<p><span>這衝突偶爾為之其實個人挺愛。</span></p>
<p><span>海鮮料依舊澎湃，除肥蝦外當中一顆大扇貝，</span></p>
<p><span>看來老闆受前東家<span><strong><a href="http://kuni.pixnet.net/blog/post/295006316-%E5%A5%BD%E5%BA%B7%E4%B9%9D%E4%B9%9D(%E5%B7%B2%E6%94%B9%E7%82%BA%E6%9C%89%E6%98%A5%E5%B0%8F%E5%90%83)%EF%BC%9A%E7%B6%93%E5%85%B8" target="_blank">好康九九</a></strong></span>的影響還是頗深。</span></p>
<p><span><strong>不過誠心希望下次來時環境能乾淨些，</strong></span></p>
<p><span><strong>今天地下室氣味有些重啊。</strong></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(奶油辣椒海鮮麵)</span></p>
