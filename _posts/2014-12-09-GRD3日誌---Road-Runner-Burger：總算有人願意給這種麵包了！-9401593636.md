<p><span>------------------------------------------------------</span><br/><span>Road Runner Burger</span><br/><span>地址：台北市大安區羅斯福路三段171號2樓</span><br/><span>電話：(02)2368-6801</span><br/><span>時間：11:30 ~ 00:00</span><br/><span>------------------------------------------------------</span></p>
<p><br/><span>信箱裡邀約不少，但其實大多不感興趣。</span></p>
<p><span>某天來自<span><strong>Road Runner</strong></span>的訊息吸引到我的目光，</span></p>
<p><span>竟剛好是粉絲團讀者。</span></p>
<p><span>漢堡類向來非我所愛，但信箱內一問一答之間愈來愈讓人好奇，</span></p>
<p><span>不禁找天中午來到師大路口。</span></p>
<p><span>地址難搞，寫著羅斯福路，店面卻在師大路裡。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(師大路的入口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 2" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9401593636/1417866202-3074808511.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><br/><span>走上二樓，裝潢簡潔，有些陽春，但還算乾淨。</span></p>
<p><span>選酪梨牛肉堡(NT.235)，加40元飲料無限暢飲，</span></p>
<p><span>商午第二套半價，點蜂蜜芥末炸雞堡。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>
<p><span>(二樓門口)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 3" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9401593636/1417866206-1496007599.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(雙人桌位)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 4" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9401593636/1417866211-752003.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(包廂型桌位)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 5" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9401593636/1417866214-2844581790.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>單點的季節性牛肉湯意外討喜(NT.60)，</span></p>
<p><span>不算濾得乾淨，但風味十足，</span></p>
<p><span>一喝就知是真材實料熬出來的。</span></p>
<p><span>但還有一項關鍵，那就是胡椒，</span></p>
<p><span>藉著添加提味達到畫龍點睛的效果。</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(無限暢飲的熱可可還不錯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 6" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9401593636/1417866220-3976695641.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(季節限定的牛肉湯)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 7" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9401593636/1417866224-2902042895.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(沙拉)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 8" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9401593636/1417866229-2170422546.jpg"/> </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>(薯條)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 9" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9401593636/1417866233-52679978.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>個人向來不愛美式漢堡，最主要是因為麵包。</span></p>
<p><span>要不就無趣的奶油包甜香，</span></p>
<p><span>或典型麥當勞，軟得塌陷，</span></p>
<p><span>或發福廚房特別烤過的脆硬，都不喜歡。</span></p>
<p><span>今天Road Runner雖仍未臻理想，但有對到胃口。</span></p>
<p><span>表皮類似bagel，真好，帶點不搶戲的嚼感。</span></p>
<p><span>肉排不算最juicy，<strong><span>但在酪梨醬護駕之下變得精彩</span><span>，</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span>一下子就吃光，這才發現原來我喜歡這種清爽型的burger呀。</span></p>
<p><span> </span></p>

<p><span>(完成度不錯的牛肉漢堡)</span></p>
<p><span><img alt="圖片 14" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kuni-tw/images/www/assets/images/9401593636/1417866252-853249414.jpg"/>  </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p><span>相較之下Road Runner的炸雞不甚理想，</span></p>
<p><span>咬下去肉汁裡藏著些許油腥味，第二口便讓人遲疑。</span></p>
<p><span>幸好還有不錯的沙拉與薯條，</span></p>
<p><span>喝完剩餘可口的牛肉湯，心曠神怡。</span></p>
<p><span>重新走回師大路，</span></p>
<p><span>享受這幾天難得一見的<span><strong>好天氣</strong></span>。</span></p>
